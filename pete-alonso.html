Biography

Pete Alonso was born in Tampa, FL on December 7, 1994. Nicknamed “Polar Bear,” he is a first baseman who made his debut in 2019 as a member of the New York Mets. He was named a reserve in the 2019 All-Star Game that season and won the 2019 MLB Home Run Derby. He beat Carlos Santana and Ronald Acuna Jr. to win the 2019 Home, Run Derby. He played both football and lacrosse as a freshman at Jesuit High School, but quit both to focus solely on baseball. He enrolled at the University of Florida and played college ball there, helping the Gators reach the 2015 and 2016 College World Series.
