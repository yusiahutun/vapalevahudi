Cindy Crawford and Rande Gerber transform into Grease lovebirds Sandy and Danny while daughter Kaia

Cindy Crawford and Rande Gerber led a cavalcade of A-list celebrities who flocked to the annual Casamigos blowout bash for Halloween, held in Los Angeles this Friday.

Rande famously co-founded the tequila brand with his pals George Clooney and Mike Meldman in 2013, and the trio remained at the head of the brand until 2017, when they sold it off to Diageo.

Despite having offloaded Casamigos, however, Rande and his 1990s Supermodel wife remain a showstopping presence at its Halloween party every year - and this weekend was no exception.

They stole the show as Danny and Sandy, the lead characters from the Broadway musical Grease, immortalized in the 1978 movie by John Travolta and Olivia Newton-John. 

With their matching black leather jackets and retro chic hairdos, Cindy and Rande were unmistakable, particularly Cindy with her extravagant blonde mane and scarlet shoes.
