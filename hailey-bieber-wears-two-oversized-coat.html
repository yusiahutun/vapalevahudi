Hailey Bieber Delivers a Master Class in Styling the Oversized Coat

On two separate occasions this week, Hailey Bieber elevated her fall uniform by simply adding a timeless coat.

Saint Laurent Long Coat in Cashmere Felt

Saint Laurent Long Coat in Cashmere Felt

While on a New York City coffee run with husband Justin Bieber yesterday, the Rhode founder looked cool in a black turtleneck, faded straight-leg jeans, black Marni loafers, and rectangular black sunglasses. She layered the look with a dark, earthy brown long coat made of cashmere felt from Saint Laurent. The double-breasted coat featured dramatically large lapels, slit pockets, and cuffed sleeves.

Meanwhile, Justin looked stylish in his own right. The "Peaches" singer wore an olive green jacket with a russet brown shearling collar, baggy black jeans, and white-and-orange sneakers. He accessorized with a mustard yellow baseball cap and oversized shades with a white frame.

Saint Laurent Long Oversize Coat in Chevron Wool

Saint Laurent Long Oversize Coat in Chevron Wool

During another outing in New York earlier this week, the model once again opted for outerwear hailing from the French fashion house. Then, she wore a speckled charcoal gray oversized coat, which included gathered sleeves and a belted waist. She styled the coat simply, wearing a light gray sweater and black jeans underneath. She finished the look with a light blue mini handbag from Bottega Veneta, black oval Saint Laurent shades, and her go-to Marni loafers.

For his part, Justin took on a more vibrant color palette, wearing a coral pink puffer jacket over a white T-shirt, black trousers, and orange-and-blue sneakers.

Hailey previously opened up about her personal style for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story.

"I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself," she said. "Even if I'm just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!"
