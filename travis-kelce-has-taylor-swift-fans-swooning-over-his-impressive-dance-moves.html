Travis Kelce has Taylor Swift fans swooning over his impressive dance moves

Taylor Swift fans can’t get over Travis Kelce’s perfect dance moves.

The world is hooked on Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce unfolding love story and it looks like the music icon has finally found a beau who can match her groovy dance moves.

Taylor Swift fans can’t get over Travis Kelce’s dance moves

Taylor Swift fans were awestruck watching Travis Kelce‘s dance moves at a charity event. The 34-year-old Kansas City Chiefs tight end participated in a charity event for Operation Breakthrough, a project to support and provide safety and education for underprivileged children.

Travis performed in a local lip-sync battle talent show, busting moves to Aretha Franklin’s 1967 hit track Respect. The short clip was more than enough to prove that the sport star turn celebrity is a natural when it comes to dancing.

“Travis can be Taylor’s backup dancer”

Taylor will never run out of a backup dancer again as her new beau could easily claim the spot, according to fans in awe of his new dance video. The enthusiasm of Taylor’s new boyfriend is also not unnoticed by Swifties as one wrote on YouTube: “It seems everything Travis does is with 110% passion & enthusiasm!”

Another smitten fan posted: “He can dance! He can sing! He can lip-sync! WOW! HE CAN EVEN PLAY FOOTBALL!!! How about that?”

Travis has one more reason to accompany Taylor on tours now as a third fan shared: “I officially think Taylor needs to take him on tour as one of her backup dancers!”

The celebrity couple is lucky to have found each other shared another Swiftie, noting: “Taylor is so lucky to have such a caring man for her boyfriend. I’ve seen other videos of him interacting with children in various events related to his foundation.”
