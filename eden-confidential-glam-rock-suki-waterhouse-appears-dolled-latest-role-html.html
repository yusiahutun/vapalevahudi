EDEN CONFIDENTIAL: Glam rock Suki Waterhouse appears all dolled up for her latest role

She burst onto the scene modelling lingerie for Marks & Spencer, but now Suki Waterhouse hopes to make it big in Hollywood. And she’s chosen the wardrobe to go with it. 

Suki stars in new Amazon Prime TV series Daisy Jones & The Six and wore two separate outfits in one day on the promotion trail.

The show is loosely inspired by Stevie Nicks and the rise of Fleetwood Mac and seems to have had a glam-rock influence on her style.

Suki, 31, the girlfriend of Twilight star Robert Pattinson, wore a Gucci embroidered pink mini dress, teamed with white go-go boots, a fur coat and oversized orange-tinted glasses. She later slipped into a pair of denim hot-pants and a white fur coat.

Heston hits boiling point at ‘disgusting’ Waitrose slur

Known as the cathedral of middle-class grocery shopping, Waitrose has become embroiled in a less-than-genteel spat with Michelin-starred chef Heston Blumenthal.

At the weekend, The Mail on Sunday disclosed that Waitrose had decided to drop the chef after a 12-year partnership which saw him starring in TV adverts and helping boost sales.

Bosses at the John Lewis-owned supermarket chain were said to have ended Blumenthal’s contract, worth ‘hundreds of thousands of pounds a year’, after tiring of his ‘unpredictability’.

It has infuriated the chef, 56, who tells me: ‘This is so disgusting.’

He says: ‘I am pleased the contract has expired.’

Chef Heston Blumenthal was dropped by Waitrose after a 12-year partnership

The comment about his alleged ‘unpredictability’ has made the chef boil over.

He suggests it’s linked to his genius for coming up with unlikely dishes such as snail porridge and egg-and-bacon ice cream.

‘Unpredictable to me is a non-linear process that turns to creativity,’ he says.

Initially focused on festive food, the Heston from Waitrose range proved hugely popular.

One year, his orange Christmas pudding was in such demand that it was being put up for sale online at 15 times its retail price after it sold out in shops.

Blumenthal tells me: ‘We are evaluating the positioning of each product that has been sold in Waitrose — some of them are our Intellectual Properties, whereas some of them are joint. Within the next six months, we plan to relaunch those products with the new brand name, for example Heston’s.’

The chef moved to France in 2019, a year after having a child with French estate agent Stephanie Gouveia, 35. In January, I disclosed that the chef, who also has three grown-up children with ex-wife Zanna, was now engaged to French businesswoman Melanie Ceysson.

 

A decisive judge on Strictly, Craig Revel Horwood is beset by hesitation when it comes to his love life. The choreographer, 58, admits his long-delayed wedding won’t take place for another couple of years.

He announced his engagement to Jonathan Myring, a gardener-turned-trainee paramedic, in 2020. ‘It might be 2025 — which is also my 60th and his 40th — to make it our 100th,’ he explains.

‘We’ve booked for 2024 but I can see us pushing it back. We want the reception at home, so the house and gardens need to look gorgeous for the wedding photos.’

 

Lloyd Webber makes sweet music with Michael Portillo at GB News

Exasperated with the BBC for turning its Young Musician Of The Year contest from the classical equivalent of The X Factor into a sideshow, Julian Lloyd Webber is joining GB News, I can disclose.

‘GB News is the only TV channel shining a light on the classical music industry in Great Britain at the moment,’ he tells me.

The celebrated cellist will appear in a new monthly slot with former Cabinet minister Michael Portillo.

‘Michael shares both my deep commitment to showcasing the talent in the industry, as well as highlighting the challenges we continue to face,’ Andrew Lloyd Webber’s brother says.

‘For many years, classical music has been side-lined from mainstream TV at the very time coverage is needed more than ever. This is a tragedy.’

Elle shows she’s a good match for new man

Having split up with her anti-vaxxer boyfriend Andrew Wakefield, Elle Macpherson is enjoying an injection of style in her life.

The supermodel known as ‘The Body’ attended the Dior show at Paris Fashion Week yesterday with her new boyfriend, the American guitarist Doyle Bramhall II, 54, and the couple made a striking impression, wearing matching camel coats and shades (pictured right).

Elle Macpherson and Doyle Bramhall II attend the Christian Dior Womenswear Fall Winter 2023-2024 show in Paris

He had introduced Elle as ‘my girlfriend’ when he took to the stage at an anti-vaxxer campaign event. 

The model, who was married to American billionaire Jeffrey Soffer until 2017, has two sons with her ex-boyfriend, the financier Arpad ‘Arki’ Busson.

This takes accumulated earnings held by him in the business to £3.5 million.
