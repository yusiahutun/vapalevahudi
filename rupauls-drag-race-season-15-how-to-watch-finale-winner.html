RuPaul's Drag Race Season 15 finale: How to watch and who is the favorite to win?

RuPaul's Drag Race moved to MTV this season after six main seasons on VH1 and eight on Logo

    RuPaul's Drag Race season 15 is coming to a close. After nearly 16 weeks of drama, delusion, and drag, we're ready to crown a winner, baby!

    As the show's milestone season draws to a close with its four finalists ready to battle it out one more time for the title of America's Next Drag Superstar, read on for what you need to know going into the grand finale.

    When is the RuPaul's Drag Race Season 15 finale?

    The season 15 finale will air on Friday, April 14, at 8 PM EST with all 16 queens of the season reuniting once again at the Ace Hotel in downtown Los Angeles.

    MORE: RuPaul Charles makes Emmys history with Drag Race win

    While the episode was filmed last week, each finalists' crowning moment is pre-filmed, with the real winner not being revealed until the episode airs.

    Where can I watch the RuPaul's Drag Race Season 15 finale?

    Drag Race moved to a new home on MTV this season, which means that you can catch the season 15 grand finale on MTV at 8/7c and on MTV.com.

    RELATED: 5 incredible TV shows coming to Netflix in April

    Other streaming services you can check out are Hulu + Live TV, Sling TV, Verizon Fios, and Philo. For those tuning in outside the US, episodes of season 15 become available on WoWPresents Plus right immediately after they air. (Avoid social media that night for spoilers!)

    Who is competing in the RuPaul's Drag Race Season 15 finale?

    The competition has been narrowed down from 16 queens to just four, with Anetra, Luxx Noir London, Mistress Isabelle Brooks, and Sasha Colby vying for the crown, a year's supply of Anastasia Beverly Hills cosmetics, and $200,000, the largest cash prize ever in the show's history.

    The four queens will each present an individual performance, following which RuPaul will choose a top two who will then lipsync for the win, similar to the season 14 finale last year.

    Who will win RuPaul's Drag Race Season 15?

    The odds-on favorite to take it all is Sasha, who came into the season with the biggest reputation as a drag legend, ending the tally with the highest number of challenge wins at four.

    READ: 5 upcoming must-watch shows you need to add to your streaming list

    Anetra is right behind her with three wins and is the season's fan favorite; she has the largest amount of fan support on the #Team posts on social media and is considered the strongest lipsyncher of the season alongside Sasha, with Michelle Visage deeming her lipsync against Marcia Marcia Marcia her favorite of all time.

    While Mistress and Luxx trail behind in terms of wins (one and two, respectively), both have dominated the edit and narrative of the season, giving them just as much a shot. Mistress has proven herself to be the one with the quickest comebacks and shady remarks, while Luxx, as a student of Drag Race, has had the most reality TV-ready package.
