Stars React to the Death of Jenni Rivera

Latin singer Jenni Rivera and six others were killed in a plane crash, the U.S. National Transportation Safety Board confirmed. The NTSB said it was dispatching help to the crash site in northern Mexico to assist in the investigation of the accident.

"On Dec. 9, 2012 at about 3:30 a.m. local time, the airplane crashed about 70 miles south of Monterrey, Mexico, due to unknown circumstances while en route to Mexico City, Mexico. All 7 persons on board were fatally injured including Latin singer Jenni Rivera," the NTSB statement read.

There was an outpouring of disbelief and grief from fans of the 43-year-old Mexican-American entertainer, who popularized the banda and nortena styles of music.
