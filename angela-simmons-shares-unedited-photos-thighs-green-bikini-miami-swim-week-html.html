Angela Simmons shares unedited photos of her 'thick thighs' in a green bikini during Miami Swim Week

Angela Simmons has earned praise for sharing unedited photos of herself proudly showing off her curves during Miami Swim Week.

The former reality star, 34, made a splash at the Matte Collection runway show on Sunday when she strutted down the catwalk at the Versace Mansion in a barely-there green string bikini. 

'Raw no edit,' she captioned images from the show on Instagram. 'REAL bodies matter and thick thighs save lives lol.'

Simmons took to Instagram after the show to shared unedited images of herself on the runway, writing, 'REAL bodies matter and thick thighs save lives'

Simmons, who is an ambassador for the body-positive swimwear brand, paired the high-waisted two-piece with a matching long-sleeve shirt that was worn open. 

Her long dark hair was styled in loose curls, and the cover-up hung off her shoulders as she walked the runway. She topped off the sultry look with layered necklaces and a pair of strappy heels.  

The Simmons Beauty founder also shared a video of herself on the catwalk, writing, 'Vegan Grown,' a nod to her plant-based diet. 

The Growing Up Hip Hop star's unedited bikini photos received more than 14,000 comments, including praise from her famous friends.
