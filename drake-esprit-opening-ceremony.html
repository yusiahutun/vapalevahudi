Drake in Esprit Tracksuit - Drake Wears Esprit Opening Ceremony Tracksuit

Congratulations: you made it to Friday and as a reward, Drake decided to step out in your favorite '80s and '90s mall brand. The rapper is the first to wear Opening Ceremony's new Esprit collection, which was announced last month.

Of the 40-piece capsule collection, Drizzy chose to go with a bright orange tracksuit with the colorful ESPRIT logo printed on both the hoodie and the sweatpants, you know to be subtle.
