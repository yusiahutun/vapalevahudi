It Evolves from Applin

To obtain an Appletun, you must first capture an Applin and then use a Tart Apple to trigger its evolution. This process transforms the small apple-shaped Pokémon into the majestic Appletun.

A: Appletun is a Grass/Dragon type Pokémon, which makes it resistant to Water, Electric, Grass, and Fighting type moves, but weak against Ice, Dragon, Fairy, and Flying type moves.
