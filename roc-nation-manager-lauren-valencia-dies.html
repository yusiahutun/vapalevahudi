Roc Nation Manager Lauren Valencia Dies

Lauren Valencia (Via Linkedin)

(CelebrityAccess) — Lauren Valencia, an artist manager at Roc Nation who guided the careers of Grimes, My Chemical Romance, and Kuk Harrell among others, has died.

A graduate of Loyola Marymount University, Valencia joined Roc Nation in 2012.

After news of her passing broke, Valencia’s former colleagues and clients offered tributes to her on social media, including Grimes, who described Valencia as the “best manager a girl could ask for.”
