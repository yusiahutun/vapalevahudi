Draya Michele, known for appearing on the show Basketball Wives, has announced she is expecting a baby. The father is NBA star Jalen Green.

The 39-year-old reality television personality shared the news on Instagram this Friday but made no mention of the 22-year-old Houston Rockets player.

Engaging her 9.6 million followers, she shared a carousel of images from a pregnancy photo shoot. 

In the caption, she wrote: 'Happy #InternationalWomensDay! As women, we navigate through so much, often leading us to question, “What is my purpose?” For me, the magic lies in motherhood and the awe-inspiring ability to bring life into this world over the span of two decades. It’s my superpower.'

Noting the sex of her unborn child, she added, 'And if anything can surpass the wonder of being a woman, it’s the privilege of bringing another woman into existence.'
