Moesha's Countess Vaughn claims boyfriend tossed bleach in her face

Moesha actress Countess Vaughn says her boyfriend David Benjamin Richard Whitten has been a domestic abuser for more than 10 years - strangling, beating, sexually abusing and threatening to kill her - and once threw bleach in her face in a rage.

The actress-singer, 40, got a protective order from a judge in Los Angeles, TMZ reported on Thursday, after she raised a number of allegations against her longtime partner.

The Idabel, Oklahoma native said Whitten threw the bleach at her two months back in an effort to injure her career, which was a sticking point for him throughout their relationship.

Whitten has also threatened to release a sex tape of the actress, she told the court.
