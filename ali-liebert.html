About

Ali Liebert is a 42 year old Canadian Actress. Born Alison Dyan Liebert on 20th August, 1981 in Surrey, British Columbia, Canada, she is famous for her roles as Rebecca in the CBC crime drama series Intelligence, Nikki Bolton in the CBS horror mystery limited series Harper's Island, Betty McRae in the Reelz/Global TV original wartime drama series Bomb Girls and its 2014 war drama TV film Bomb Girls: Facing the Enemy, Principal Grey in the Disney Channel/Disney XD science fiction adventure comedy series Mech-X4, Ms. Petosa in the 2017 coming-of-age family drama film Wonder, Darcy Archer in the 2017 Hallmark Channel original Christmas romantic drama TV film A Gift to Remember and its 2019 sequel film Cherished Memories: A Gift to Remember 2, Kelly in the 2018 Hallmark Channel original romantic comedy TV film Cooking with Love, and Natalia in the 2020 drama film They Who Surround Us. Her zodiac sign is Leo.
