Its Gary Levoxs House!

Gary LeVox of Rascal Flatts had this house built after acquiring the 2.6-acre property in 2005. The 12,800 square-foot residence includes 18 total rooms. That's five bedrooms, six full bathrooms and two half-baths, plus a bunch of others. Inside you'll enjoy walking on marble floors (as well as carpet and tile), while outside you'll enjoy gorgeous views of surrounding forests.

There are two two-car garages and four fireplaces inside this Mitchell Barnett-designed Nashville mansion. Real Estalker points out that LeVox bought the land over eight years ago for $295,000. At the official realtor website, the house is listed as being built one year later. Double family rooms, a home theater, fitness room and swimming pool all come with the property, as do the fabric ceilings, which one assumes anyone who has the cash for this castle can afford to have professionally cleaned. Otherwise, you'll need a tall ladder.
