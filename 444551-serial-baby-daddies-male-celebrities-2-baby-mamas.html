Serial Baby Daddies: Male Celebrities Known to Have More than 2 Baby Mamas

  • TUKO.co.ke compiled a list of famous people from Kenya and abroad who could be considered serial baby daddies

TUKO.co.ke highlighted some of the famous names with more than two baby mamas.
