Every Product She Uses for Glowing Skin

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

Jennifer Lopez can sing, act, and dance like the queen she is, but boy can she do her makeup, too. It’s not every day that fans get a glimpse into her most-loved beauty products, so it’s only fair that we put such things in writing so we can all get that J.Lo glow.

The beauty icon did a GRWM video with Vogue and to no surprise, she used a lot of JLo Beauty products to prep her skin. But look beyond her own brand’s formulas and you realize Jenny from the Block is just like you and me. She loves NARS, Anastasia Beverly Hills, Fenty Beauty, Chanel, and several other popular names. You’ll even spot a $21 lip gloss, $24 lip liner, $28 mascara, and $22 blush in her makeup lineup. We’ll always say yes to celeb-backed, affordable picks!

Get ready to order all of Jennifer Lopez’s makeup products so you, too, can perfect her glowing skin beat.

  • Concealer

    NARS Radiant Creamy Concealer

    In the YouTube GRWM, J.Lo swipes on NARS’ Radiant Creamy Concealer anywhere that needs brightening, such as under her eyes and around her nose. It’s a long-wearing, buildable, medium coverage, radiant finish liquid concealer that stays on for up to 16 hours, per the brand.

    With over 8,000 five-star reviews on Sephora, one shopper wrote, “I have tried nearly every concealer Sephora has and this one’s my holy grail. It’s so lightweight, creamy, buildable, and does not crease! it has a radiant finish and doesn’t cling onto dry patches, which I love. Seriously don’t think, just buy!”

    Later on, while the singer is contouring, she whips out another concealer. She uses YSL’s Touche Éclat pen down the bridge of her nose, under her eyes, and on a couple of other spots where she wants to illuminate.

  • Foundation

    Loved by Hailey Bieber, Kendall Jenner, and J.Lo (she says she’s been mixing three different shades “for years”), this Chanel foundation is super hard to find in stock anywhere and has actually been out of stock at Chanel for a long, long time. Thankfully, it’s back in stock so you can cop the formula that gives you the most beautiful, dewiest complexion.

  • Eyeshadow

    Tom Ford Eye Color Quad Eyeshadow Palette

    Lopez keeps it simple with a neutral-toned Tom Ford eyeshadow quad. She uses three brown shades to create a natural-looking eye.

    However, she isn’t the only fan of this palette. One Sephora reviewer raved, “This product completely changed my eyeshadow game. When applied with fingers, the matte shades feel like velvet. The shades are also highly pigmented and are the best shadows I have ever used. They are a staple in every look. Once you’ve used them, your standards for shadows will go sky-high. Best shadows. Ever.”

  • Blush

    I would’ve never guessed the singer uses a $22 Beautyblender blush to achieve her signature sun-kissed glow. “I like cream blush, especially when you get a little older,” J.Lo says in the Vogue video. She likes the shade Playful Peach so much that she used it in her last movie.

    “This product is one of the best. It blends into the skin for a natural-looking blush. Blends effortlessly and lasts all day,” wrote one Sephora shopper. “Can we mention [it’s] affordable?”

  • Lip Liner

    Make Up For Ever Artist Color Pencil Brow, Eye & Lip Liner

    J.Lo and Mrs. Bieber just keep using the same makeup products. Here’s another one: Make Up For Ever’s brow, eye, and lip liner. The singer says “I’m obsessed, it’s my new fave” as she outlines her pout in her Beauty Secrets video and shows viewers a technique she learned from social media.

    The multifunctional pencil has a 4.4-star overall rating on Sephora, with one happy shopper saying, “My favorite lip liner EVER. Wherever Walnut is the perfect shade for me. Has great wearing time and doesn’t come off even after applying gloss. Worth the hype.”

  • Lip Gloss

    Fenty Beauty Gloss Bomb Universal Lip Luminizer

    It seems like everyone and their grandmas is a fan of Fenty Beauty’s Gloss Bomb. Add Lopez to the long, long list of stans because she uses multiple shades of this lip gloss. She applies a warmer, orange-peach shade all over her lips, and sometimes she’ll go in with a light pink shade to add a little extra dimension to her pout (not that she needs it; her pout is to die for).

    This lip product is a Sephora favorite, boasting over 9,000 five-star reviews. One shopper raved, “The most beautiful lip gloss I’ve ever tried. My jaw genuinely dropped when I swatched it for the first time. NEED.”

  • Brow Pencil

    On Sale % off

    I couldn’t be more envious of J.Lo’s arches, which she fills in with Anastasia Beverly Hills’ Brow Whiz. One end has the pencil and the other has a spoolie. In the GRWM, you can see the singer brush her brow hairs up and then fill them in.

    Some shoppers love the pencil so much that they keep coming back to it. One Sephora reviewer wrote, “I have been through a few different brow pencils now and I must say this one has been my favorite! It blends out nicely and the color matched my natural brows perfectly.”

  • Setting Powder

    Make Up For Ever Ultra HD Microfinishing Loose Powder

    A makeup look is almost never complete without dabbing some setting powder under your eyes and on your T-zone (where you tend to get oily). J.Lo opts for this Make Up For Ever loose powder, which is ultra-blurring and light-diffusing, per the brand.

    If this shopper review doesn’t sell you on this product, I don’t know what will: “Tried many over the years and I always come back to this! Super fine translucent powder and does not look cakey at all! Trust this for fine lines and setting under the eyes. Also takes the shine away from [the] T-zone [and] keeps the base look[ing] super natural. Matte finish, no cake, no coverage, and no color. Perfect for [the] last step [of] your makeup routine. I don’t experience any white flashbacks. A little goes a long way!”

  • Mascara

    IT Cosmetics Superhero Mascara

    On Sale % off

    My ears perked up when J.Lo said in the YouTube video, “This is the most important part.” The essential step she’s talking about is her mascara. She glides IT Cosmetics’ Superhero Mascara through her lashes and the result is fluttery perfection.

    “This mascara feels like nothing. Nothing. It makes my straight, fine, wimpy lashes full and long. I no longer feel like I should get extensions,” one Amazon reviewer wrote. “It doesn’t end up under my eyes. It doesn’t flake. It washes off easily. I couldn’t be more surprised or impressed. Love it!”
