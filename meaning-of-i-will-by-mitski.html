Meaning of I Will by Mitski

In Mitski's song "I Will," the overall theme revolves around the concept of care and support in a relationship. The lyrics express a deep devotion and willingness to take care of the other person, highlighting the significance of emotional vulnerability and the strength that can be found in providing comfort to someone you love.One standout lyric that contributes to the development of this theme is "I will see your body bare and still I will live here." This line emphasizes the importance of acceptance and unconditional love. It suggests that despite any flaws or vulnerabilities, the speaker is committed to remaining present and supportive, solidifying the idea of a profound and enduring connection.Another important lyric is "And all the quiet nights you bear, seal them up with care. No one needs to know they're there, for I will hold them for you." This lyric centers around the idea of emotional weight and carrying each other's burdens. It suggests that in a healthy relationship, both parties are willing to take on the emotional struggles of the other person and provide a safe space for them to express themselves without fear of judgment. It emphasizes the importance of trust and creating an environment where vulnerability is celebrated.Moving onto the bridge, the lyric "‘Cause all I ever wanted is here, all I ever wanted. All I want is always you, it's always you" brings forth the theme of contentment and finding fulfillment in the presence of the other person. It implies that the speaker's desires and happiness are intrinsically tied to their partner, emphasizing the depth of their feelings and the profound impact the other person has on their life. This theme of intertwining destinies further strengthens the notion of care and unconditional support.Lastly, in the chorus, the line "And while you sleep, I'll be scared. So by the time you wake, I'll be brave" introduces the theme of resilience and the strength that can be found in supporting someone through their vulnerabilities. It acknowledges that fear and uncertainty are natural parts of life, but by sharing those fears with each other, they can draw strength and determination to face whatever challenges come their way. It reinforces the importance of being a pillar of support for the other person, helping them overcome their fears and insecurities.Overall, "I Will" delves deep into the theme of care and support within a relationship, showcasing the power of vulnerability and the strength that lies in providing comfort to someone you love. The song's standout lyrics explore the shared burdens, unconditional love, intertwining destinies, and resilience that contribute to a healthy and fulfilling partnership.
