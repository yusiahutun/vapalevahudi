Diego Dreyfus (born December 23, 1978 in Mexico, DF) is a Mexican Entrepreneur, Engineer, Actor, and Life Coach, though he does not like the term "coaching". He sees himself as a person who systematizes emotional processes and has the ability to communicate it to people. His acting career started when he was 7. He stopped acting to become a businessman, making his first million at the age of 27. He is well-known in Latin America for his brand "Te Vas A Morir", which includes conferences, 1-on-1 coachings on YouTube, physical and digital events, a blog, "La Escuela de la Vida" (digital educational courses on Love, Money, Success, and Uniqueness). He also has a podcast, and a book.
