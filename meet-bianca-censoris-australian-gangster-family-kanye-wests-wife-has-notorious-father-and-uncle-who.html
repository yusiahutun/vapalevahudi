Meet Bianca Censoris Australian gangster family: Kanye Wests wife has a notorious father

Here’s what we’ve heard about Bianca Censori’s Aussie clan.

Who are Bianca Censori parents?

Not much is known about Bianca’s pre-Kanye-West life, but her father is reportedly a notorious gangster named Elia “Leo” Censori. Per UK media, he was jailed in 1982 for possession of a pistol and ammunition.

Meanwhile, Bianca’s uncle is believed to be Eris Censori, a “notorious gangland killer” who was once dubbed “Melbourne’s Al Capone”, according to British media. The same reports claims that Eris was sentenced to death in Western Australia for murder, but it was later commuted to life imprisonment. Bianca’s other uncle Edmondo is also said to have been involved in crime. The British press reports note that Bianca’s family originated from Italy, before settling in Australia.

Bianca’s mother Alexandra, meanwhile, seems to be more low-key and has been described as having a close physical resemblance to her daughter.

Her glam “Kardashian-like” sisters

In fact, just like Kim’s mum Kris Jenner and her sisters Kourtney, Khloé, Kendall and Kylie, Bianca’s sisters and mother also have names beginning with the same letter – Alexandra, Alyssia and Angelina.

Bianca’s sister Angelina told Australian newspaper The Herald Sun that she was supportive of her sibling’s relationship with the US rapper, saying: “It’s very exciting news for both my sister and the family but we choose to have some privacy for the time being.” Alyssia added that she was “super happy for them both”.

What does Bianca Censori do?

As reported by Elle, Bianca, 29, is an architect who has worked at Yeezy since 2020. Per her LinkedIn, she obtained a bachelor’s and master’s in architecture from the University of Melbourne.

Has Kanye West met the Censoris?

West met his Aussie in-laws last July in Tokyo, according to reports in British media.

How did Bianca Censori meet Kanye West?

According to Parade, one of Censori’s friends revealed that West slid into her DMs. The pal added that West told Censori to “come and work for me” and she moved to Los Angeles to take him up on the offer. Before that it seems she was enjoying living and working in Melbourne.

“The pandemic was the first time in a long time a lot of Australian creatives were brought back to one place. Melbourne felt like this melting pot of global creativity,” she told Vogue Australia of the period. “People began to cross disciplines and collaborate in ways I hadn’t experienced before.”

The pair wed in December 2022, a month after West’s divorce from Kardashian was finalised, and reportedly tied the knot in a private ceremony.

Does Kim Kardashian like Bianca Censori?

It’s unclear exactly how the Skims billionaire feels about her ex’s new spouse, but an article in the US media has suggested she wasn’t a fan from the beginning because she suspected chemistry between the two while she and West were still together.

What’s more, Censori has been hanging out with Kardashian and West’s children. Photographed holding hands with Censori in June last year, North West reportedly said she found her new stepmum “super cool”, according to Grazia.

It hasn’t escaped people’s notice that Kardashian and Censori also share a striking physical resemblance and fashion sense.
