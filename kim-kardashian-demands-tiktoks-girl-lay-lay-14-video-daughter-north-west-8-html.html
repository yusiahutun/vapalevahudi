Kim Kardashian 'demands' TikTok's That Girl Lay Lay, 14, take down video with daughter North West, 8

TikTok star That Girl Lay Lay called out Kim Kardashian for allegedly demanding she take down a music video featuring her now eight-year-old daughter North West but she and estranged husband Kanye West were never asked for permission according to a new report.

That Girl Lay Lay had posted a video featuring the eldest daughter of Kim and Kanye West, who was six at the time, during a playdate last year featuring music from one of her new rap songs.

The teenager - who just landed her own Nickelodeon series - went on to claim on social media that Kim, 40, texted her forcing her to take down the clip without explanation after having previously invited her to their home.
