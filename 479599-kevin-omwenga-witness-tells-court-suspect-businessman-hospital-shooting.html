Kevin Omwenga: Witness Tells Court Suspect Followed Businessman to Hospital after Shooting

  • Kelvin Omwenga was shot dead on Friday, August 21, 2020, inside his house in Kilimani in a suspected business deal gone wrong
  • Businessman Robert John Ouko alongside Chris Philip Okeyo were linked to the shooting and later charged with murder
  • A witness told the High Court that the accused lured the victim into a room in their apartment before shooting him

A witness has detailed how the suspected killers of the controversial businessman Kelvin Omwenga Ombati executed it in Nairobi's Kilimani area.

Omwenga was shot dead on Friday, August 21, 2020, inside his house in what detectives suspect could have resulted from a business deal gone sour.

Businessman Robert John Ouko alongside Chris Philip Okeyo, would then be implicated in the murder of the youthful man and charged.

On Tuesday, October 25, a witness, Kelvin Amenya, said that the accused lured the victim into a room in their apartment before shooting him.

Amenya further testified before the High Court that the suspect emerged from the room holding a pistol while shouting that the Omwenga had shot himself.

Suspected killers followed him to hospital

After the shooting, the witness and the caretaker drove the deceased to Nairobi Women's Hospital.

Amenya said the first accused, Ouko, left the apartment while they were looking for an ambulance. He later saw him at the hospital where the victim was pronounced dead.

When the witness went to Kilimani Police Station to report the incident, he found the accused pleading innocent over the killing.

The witness said he confronted the accused in front of DCIO officers on the whereabouts of the pistol he had used in the murder.

Upon a thorough search, the weapon would be recovered at the house where Omwenga was killed.

KSh 100m payment

As previously reported, Omwenga was set to travel to Dubai for KSh 100 million payment in a business deal whose details are scanty.

The deceased was to travel to the Arab country in the company of his friend and neighbour, only identified as Jamal.

The Dubai trip failed to materialise after the overnight millionaire met his death at his apartment in Galana Suites.

Also, messages retrieved from the deceased's phone showed he had communicated to his shooter who thanked him for the unknown assistance that Omwenga had offered him.
