Onyx Kelly's parents

Onyx Kelly's parents, Iggy Azalea and Playboi Carti, met sometime in 2018 while Carti was on tour abroad. By the end of 2018, the couple had moved in together. In 2019, Iggy and Carti revealed in an interview that they had moved to Atlanta, living in Playboi Carti's hometown.

Who is Playboi Carti?

Playboi Carti, real name Jordan Terrell Carter, is an American rapper. He was born on 13 September 1996 and turns 27 on 13 September 2023. He began his career in 2011, but his breakthrough came in 2017 with his self-titled mix tape.
