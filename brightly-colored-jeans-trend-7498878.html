How to Wear the Brightly Colored Jeans Trend

Brightly colored jeans come in and out of fashion, and right now, they're firmly back at the top of the denim pyramid. No shade to blue, but brands are finally offering a wide range of colors, from vivid hues to softly saturated pastel jeans — and we're here for all the options. Whether you live out loud in technicolor or prefer your wardrobe whispers instead of roars, there's no reason not to give anything but blue-colored jeans a go.

One of the best things about colorful jeans is that they serve maximum fashion with minimal effort on your part — all you have to do is slip on a pair. That said, they're also surprisingly simple to tone down — or amp up! — with wardrobe basics or statement pieces you likely already have in your closet.

Ahead, 10 colorful denim jeans outfits to inspire your summer style.

With a Graphic Tee

Pairing colorful denim bottoms with a faded graphic tee is a great way to dip your toe into this vibrant trend without diving headfirst into making a saturated statement shade. White sneakers and a deep-toned handbag also add dimension to the color palette.

With a Neutral Crop Top

Yes, even minimalist fashionistas can wear brightly colored jeans. For a streamlined look, look no further than a ribbed tank in a neutral tone. A shoulder bag in a similarly soft neutral will balance out even the most technicolor of high-waisted jeans.

With a Matching Jean Jacket

If you love a bold look, you'll likely be asking yourself why you don't already own a brightly colored Canadian tuxedo set — but don't beat yourself up. Instead, start planning how you'll style it with a neon-patterned clash-on-purpose handbag.

With a Denim Bra

One way to mitigate how overwhelming a matching colorful denim set can be is to simply put together an outfit with less fabric, like this lime green jean and denim bra get-up. If shade-matching isn't your strength, don't worry. A complementary color works just as well.

Color-Block with a Crop Top

A bold approach to brightly colored jeans, colorblock denim makes a major statement that benefits from a quieter neutral top. To get the look, try something like Warp + Weft NCE Wide Leg Jeans in Two Faced.

With a Striped Top

For a look that's a bit punk and a lot rock, pair jeans in a moody red or deep purple with a top that features a racing stripe. If you're opting for a jacket, we recommend keeping things simple underneath with a black cami.

With a Colorblock Button-Down

Another way to diffuse the impact of colorful jeans without diluting their fashion power is to color-block on top. Grab an oversized button-down that features similar levels of saturation — rather than an exact shade match — for a look that's cohesive without being overwhelming.

With a Sheer, Patterned Top

If true colorblock jeans feel a shade too adventurous — so to speak — try their toned-down style twin: a pair of watercolor-washed jeans. Rather than highly saturated and contrasting colors, water colorblocking creates a softer, ombré effect by blending similar shades together. It's a perfect pant to wear with a vibrant, patterned top.

With Contrasting Heels

Elevate a pair of straight-leg colored jeans by wearing them with a pair of romantic bow-tipped low heels. The blend of street style and elegance is a fashion match made in heaven.

With an Oversized Blazer

Aim for a close but not true match with everyone's favorite piece of XXL outerwear — the oversized blazer. Since the outfit itself is such a statement, feel free to keep accessories like bags and sneakers neutral.
