Khlo Kardashian and Lamar Odom victims of jewellery robbery

    Khloé Kardashian suffered a set-back this week after becoming the victim of a $250,000 jewellery robbery.Law enforcement sources have revealed to that someone stole watches and gold chains from the Tarzana home Khloé shared with her estranged husband Lamar Odom.

    The mansion has been sat empty for a month now, and police reportedly believe it is an inside job since there were no signs of forced entry.Khloé filed for divorce from Lamar in December, and the house has since been sold to actress Kaley Cuoco and her husband Ryan Sweeting.

    Khloé looked stunning in a red velvet dress at Elton John's post-Oscar bash

    The theft was discovered on Monday night, when Khloé and her mother Kris were cleaning out the home for the new owners. It came 24 hours after the 29-year-old's glamorous appearance at Elton John's AIDS Foundation Oscar-viewing party.Khloé joined her sisters Kim Kardashian, Kourtney Kardashian, Kourtney's partner Scott Disick and Kardashian matriarch Kris Jenner for the bash at West Hollywood Park on Sunday.

    Khloé was accompanied to the party by her mother Kris and sisters Kim and Kourtney

    The brunette looked striking in a burgundy velvet wrap gown, her hair styled into voluminous curls. She vamped up her look with a gothic lipstick.

    Mum-of-one Kim, 33, chose a plunging red ball gown, while Kourtney, 34, looked chic in an on-trend black suit, featuring a sequined jacket and necktie.
