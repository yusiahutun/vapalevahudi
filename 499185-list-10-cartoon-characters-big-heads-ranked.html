List of 10 best cartoon characters with big heads, ranked

Cartoon characters have been depicted as having magnified features like big heads and weird shapes for many reasons. The reasons can be artistic or practical hence creating a visual language that is understood by viewers. Cartoon characters with big heads help emphasise facial expressions and add humour.

The face is an essential part of a cartoon character because it is what the audience looks at to interpret their emotions and intentions. Some of the most memorable cartoon characters have big heads and small body sizes. Tom and Jerry is an excellent example of characters with big heads.

The two characters feature big-headed mice and cats constantly getting into mischief. And although Tom and Jerry have been on the screens for years, it is easy to recognise the characters because of their facial features.

1. Boss Baby

Boss Baby is a big-head cartoon on Nickelodeon. The character appears in television series with a similar name. The personality is a baby, but something makes him unique. He is an agent working for BabyCorp. He has a disproportionately large head compared to his body.

His large head makes him look serious and comical, perfectly fitting his double life as a baby and a secret agent. Additionally, Boss Baby has large, round, piercing eyes that convey a sense of intelligence and determination and are often used to express a range of emotions, from curiosity to frustration to triumph.

2. Bubbles, Blossom, and Buttercup

Bubbles, Blossom, and Buttercup are big head female cartoon characters from the animated TV series The Powerpuff Girls. These three characters have a unique head shape that helps distinguish them from one another. For example, Bubbles has a round, large head compared to her body, which gives her a more childlike appearance.

6. Mr Bean

Mr Bean is commonly known for his facial expressions and behaviour that are unique to his head design. He has a round head that is long towards the bottom. His short, dark brown hair is usually combed forward messily, emphasising his dishevelled appearance.

His oval-shaped face features prominent, expressive eyebrows and distinctively animated eyes that move in a somewhat jerky fashion, giving him a humorous and quirky appearance.

7. Shin Chan

8. SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob is one of the most beloved big-headed characters in the history of cartoons. He features a yellow sponge head with blue eyes. 9. Stewie Griffin

Stewie is one of the best male characters with big foreheads. The character is from the television series Family Guy. Griffin's head is elliptical-shaped with a pronounced football shape bent forward.

10. Timmy Turner

Timmy Turner is the main character of the animated television show The Fairly Odd Parents. His head design is simple yet distinctive, featuring an oval-shaped head with spiky, light pink hair.
