Brown infamously caused a scene during a January 2, 2022 game against the New York Jets, undressing and tossing equipment before rushing off the field. He was subsequently released by the Bucs and has not returned to the NFL since.

After being booted off the Bucs, Brown turned on his pal Brady and said the NFL quarterback was formerly only a friend to him because he was good at football.

Earlier this month, Brown released a text message purportedly from Brady dating back to May of 2021.

'You are demonstrating very poor decision and poor communication to so many people who have gone above and beyond to help you,' began the message, which Brown shared on Snapchat.

The name at the top of the message reads 'TOM BRADY' and the text appears to have been sent on May 10, 2021 at 12:58pm.

In May of 2021, Brown had not yet run afoul of the Buccaneers. The two were still teammates coming of a Super Bowl victory and Brown was seven months away from his famously bizarre exit.

More recently, Brown has been attacking Brady on social media over his divorce from model Gisele Bundchen.
