The Best Wedding Dress Styles for Your Zodiac Sign

You already know that your zodiac sign can help you figure out what swimsuit trend to try, what beauty products to buy, and what kind of bag you need in your life, but did you also know it can determine what wedding dress will most suit you? Going off qualities that each sign is known for, we've chosen a few unique, special finds meant to enhance that sign's inner energy, allowing them to shine their brightest on their big day.

Keep scrolling for the best wedding dresses for your zodiac sign.

Aries

As one of the sportier zodiac signs, Aries might want to opt for a jumpsuit instead of a traditional dress, much like this design by Sachin and Babi ($800). Of course, that doesn't mean it won't be blinged out with sequins, lace, or crystals, making it fancy enough for a black-tie event and casual for a low-key ceremony/reception. Some major benefits of wearing pants on their big day? This ram will be able to run around their party, dance freely, and greet guests easily in a jumpsuit.

(Note: This product is currently sold out; for something similar, we recommend Katya Top and Embellished Whitely Pants).

Taurus

This style is pure old-school Hollywood elegance, which is the perfect look for Taurus who is known for their glitzy sentiments. If the Bull opts for this style in chiffon or lace with a sweeping train, they'll be radiating serious Veronica Lake and Lena Horne vibes from the 1940s — especially with their hair down and parted to the side or styles short with flowers. This $96 option from Lulu's definitely nails it.

Gemini

Being that Geminis are the twin sign of the zodiac, it makes sense for them to opt for separates that pair perfectly together, like ASOS's lace set ($200). A two-piece outfit consisting of a slightly cropped bateau top and full skirt will make for a casual yet sophisticated look that they can wear for an afternoon, twilight, or evening affair.

(Note: This product is currently sold out; for something similar, we recommend Little Mistress Bridal Maxi Dress).

Cancer

Cancers might find themselves gravitating toward sweetheart dress styles since the design secretly speaks volumes about their emotions. Not only is the top half curved to look like a heart — a classic and romantic detail, which hints toward their innermost sentiments — it'll also help show off that part of the body (AKA their décolletage) for something more literal. David's Bridal has a wide selection of options, many of which are under $1,000.

Leo

It's common knowledge that Leos love to make bold statements; they simply enjoy the drama and flair of making a theatrical entrance. A ball gown is the best way for this lion to let everyone know that they're the one that all eyes should be fixated on (ok, ok, fine — them and their significant other). With a voluminous, statement piece like this, Leo will also live up to its reputation of being the most regal sign of the zodiac, while ensuring they shine all night long.

Virgo

Virgos tend to prefer less frills and a simpler style of clothing, and the same goes for the dress they'll wear while walking down the aisle. However, the earth sign will also benefit from mixing an easy, effortlessly glam creation with a skin-baring twist, meaning a timeless, backless style that fits like a glove — much like this pick from Shona Joy — will be the perfect choice.

Libra

An elegant, empire waist dress will ensure that Libra feels like their fairytale romance and happily ever after ending is finally here. The addition of crystals, lace, or fine materials — anything to help make their flowy dress look like a piece of art that transcends time — will help the air sign create their ultimate wedding day vibe. We're partial's to Torrid's $198 design.

Scorpio

Although Scorpio is known to have intense energy, when it comes to their wedding gown, a simpler look, like Reformation's Tullamore Dress ($528), is best. The reason? A slip dress can radiate the sleek yet seductive vibes they're aiming for without going overboard. Plus, there's no such thing as basic for Scorpio. This sign will harness their passionate nature with the little extras, like wearing deep red lipstick or nail polish or adding roses to their too-cool look.

(Note: This product is currently sold out; for a similar style, we recommend Joana Silk Dress).

Sagittarius

Being that Sagittarius rules the hips and thighs, a trumpet-style dress is calling their name. It accentuates their hips and flares out mid-thigh to the ankles, which means it's not only breathtaking to look at (like this Justin Alexander pick) but easy to move in. The cut will no doubt come in handy when this sign hits the dance floor, and will only add to the fun of their big day (the most important detail).

Capricorn

The sea-goat favors more traditional looks, which is why they'll lean towards wearing a dress that features flowing bell sleeves (usually made from lace or chiffon), like this Maggie Sottero design, which will keep them warm while honoring the vintage aesthetic that this sign tends to love. The good thing about this dress style is that Capricorn can either lean into the throwback aesthetic or make it feel fresh and modern, depending on how they accessorize and the fabric they pick.

Aquarius

When it comes to fashion, Aquarius marches to the beat of their own drum due to their unique nature. Their wedding will be fashion-forward and veer from tradition — which is exactly what will make it exciting. Obviously, we can't expect the water-bearer to go with anything too generic, so a plunging V-neck wedding dress in an unconventional color — whether it's pink, like this Bronx and Banco option, black, or gold — is our pick for what will best align with their wedding vision.

Pisces

Is there a better match for a water sign than a mermaid-style strapless dress, like this one from Azazie? Pisces are symbolized by fish, so it totally makes sense for them to wear a dress that physically depicts their innate and transcendent state of being. The dreamy and mystical design will make Pisces feel as though they are floating through the air — or rather, walking on water — as they make their way down the aisle.

(Note: This product is currently sold out; for something similar, we recommend David's Bridal Collection Organza Mermaid Wedding Dress).
