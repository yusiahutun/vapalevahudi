Fireworks to light up Ellisville, Laurel | Local News

Explosions will be rocking the Free State starting Sunday with the All-American Red, White and Blue Star Spangled Fourth of July Celebration in Ellisville.

West Ellisville Baptist Church will host the event at 6 p.m. on the Jones County Junior College practice facility. There will be food, games, fun and music, plus a salute to military personnel.
