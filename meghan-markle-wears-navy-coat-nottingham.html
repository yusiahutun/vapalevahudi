Meghan Markle stuns in Mackage navy coat on very first royal engagement

Prince Harry’s new fiancée wows in latest outfit

    Prince Harry and his new bride-to-be-Meghan Markle travelled to Nottingham on 1 December, for their first royal visit together since announcing their engagement. The former actress looked radiant as she walked alongside the Prince, dressed in a black roll-neck and cream skirt combo, which she teamed with a navy coat by Mackage, boots and a burgundy handbag by Strathberry. She wore her long raven hair loosely around her shoulders, while natural-looking make-up perfectly enhanced her natural beauty.

    Meghan stuns in navy coat by Mackage

    The city was overrun with crowds of people, members of the press and photographers ahead of the couple's visit. Harry and his new fiancée, 36, smiled happily as they spoke with members of the public during their various engagements in the city; they made their appearance in conjunction with World Aids Day, and are attending a charity fair, which is being hosted by the Terrance Higgins trust.

    Meghan braves the warm

    It's been an exceptionally busy week for the happy couple following their engagement announcement on Monday. Shortly after appearing in front of the press for an official photocall, Meghan's engagement outfit had completely sold out, with fans desperate to get their hands on her Line the Label white coat, bottle green dress by P.A.R.O.S.H and shoes by Aquazzura - the coat and dress have since been re-named, both called 'The Meghan' in her honour.
