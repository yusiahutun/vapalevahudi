Sherri Shepherd Pics Hollywood Life

Sherri Shepherd has had quite a varied and accomplished career as an actress and TV personality. She made her debut in the 1995 sitcom ‘Cleghorne.’ She went on to land roles in plenty of other popular sitcoms, including recurring parts on ‘Everybody Loves Raymond’ and ‘The Jamie Foxx Show.’ She also went on to act in many acclaimed sitcoms into the 2000s and 2010s, including shows like ’30 Rock’ and ‘How I Met Your Mother,’ both of which she had recurring parts on.

Other than her acting work, she’s also a beloved TV personality. She was a guest co-host on ‘The View’ from 2007 to 2014, and has returned to guest co-host on multiple occasions. She’s also taken part in reality competitions like ‘The Masked Singer,’ and ‘Dancing With The Stars,’ where she competed in seasons 2 and 14, respectively.
