The Early Days

Shakin Stevens, born Michael Barrett, is a Welsh singer and songwriter who shot to fame in the 1980s. His career started in the late 1960s, when he formed his first band called the Sunsets. However, it wasn’t until the 1980s that he achieved widespread success, becoming one of the UK’s top-selling artists of the decade.

A Guinness World Record Holder

Did you know that Shakin Stevens holds a Guinness World Record? He achieved this feat by being the first artist to have a top 40 hit in the UK charts for six consecutive decades. From the 1970s to the 2010s, his songs consistently charted, proving his enduring popularity.

His Chart-Topping Success

Shakin Stevens is best known for his chart-topping hits. In fact, he has had four number one singles in the UK, including “This Ole House,” “Green Door,” “Oh Julie,” and “Merry Christmas Everyone.” These songs became anthems of the 80s and remain popular to this day.

A Star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame

Shakin Stevens has left an indelible mark on the music industry, and his contributions have been recognized with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. This prestigious honor is a testament to his enduring influence and talent.

Sold-Out Tours

As a testament to his popularity, Shakin Stevens has embarked on numerous sold-out tours throughout his career. Fans from around the world flock to see him perform his iconic hits live, making each concert a memorable experience.

A Multilingual Performer

One fascinating fact about Shakin Stevens is his ability to sing in multiple languages. He has released albums and performed in languages such as Spanish, Swedish, and Danish, showcasing his versatility as an artist.

A Versatile Musician

Shakin Stevens is not only known for his singing but also for his musical talent. He is proficient in playing several instruments, including the guitar, piano, and drums. His musical prowess adds depth and richness to his performances.

A Successful Christmas Song

Shakin Stevens’ hit single “Merry Christmas Everyone” has become synonymous with the holiday season. Released in 1985, the song continues to be a beloved Christmas classic that is played year after year.

An Accomplished Songwriter

A Living Legend

A Household Name

Influencing Future Artists

Shakin Stevens’ unique style and sound have influenced many artists who came after him. His fusion of rockabilly, country, and pop created a fresh and distinctive sound that resonated with audiences and left a lasting impact.

The Rebirth of Rockabilly

Shakin Stevens brought rockabilly music back into the mainstream during the 80s. His revival of this genre sparked a renewed interest in the music of artists like Elvis Presley, Carl Perkins, and Jerry Lee Lewis.

Shaky, released in 1981, became one of Shakin Stevens’ biggest successes. The album topped the charts in the UK and spawned multiple hit singles, including “Oh Julie” and “You Drive Me Crazy.

FAQs

1. How did Shakin Stevens get his stage name?

Shakin Stevens was born Michael Barratt, but he adopted his stage name early on in his career. The name “Shakin” was inspired by his energetic performances, while “Stevens” was a tribute to his musical idol, Eddie Cochran.

2. What is Shakin Stevens’ most famous song?

Without a doubt, Shakin Stevens’ most famous song is “Merry Christmas Everyone.” Released in 1985, the catchy holiday tune has become a Christmas classic, topping the charts in the UK and making its way into many people’s festive playlists.

3. How many records has Shakin Stevens sold?

Shakin Stevens has sold over 30 million records worldwide. His chart success, particularly in the 1980s, made him one of the best-selling artists of his time.

4. Has Shakin Stevens won any awards?

Yes, Shakin Stevens has been recognized for his contributions to the music industry. He has won numerous awards, including several British Breakthrough Artist of the Year awards and a BRIT Award for Best British Male Solo Artist.

5. Is Shakin Stevens still performing today?

Yes, Shakin Stevens is still actively performing and touring. Despite his long and successful career, he continues to captivate audiences with his electrifying stage presence and timeless music.
