Breaking Down the Best &amp; Worst Dressed of the Week with Eva Marie!

The good, the bad, the Beyonce.

This week, "Total Divas" star/designer Natalie Eva Marie switched up her own look -- going from her fiery red mane to a jet black 'do -- to help rate the hits and misses of the VMAs. Joining her are toofab's very own Candice Brock and Katie Hayes, as well as celebrity stylist Salvadaor Camarena.

Knowles commanded the stage at the MTV awards show, where she performed a nearly-16 minute medley of songs from "Lemonade" and picked up Video of the Year. And while she crushed it inside, her red carpet look was definitely up for debate. While team toofab bowed down to Bey, one panelist said her sheer, feather-adorned Francesco Scognamiglio gown was a little too "Broadway musical" for the event.

There was no deliberation on Britney Spears' Julien MacDonald dress, which showed off the perfect amount of skin, while also keeping it classy. Another look that got "Fab" across the board: the green fringe jumpsuit Rihanna wore to the after-parties following the show.

Of course, not every look earned rave reviews. Kim Kardashian was called out for not going sexy enough ... and Cassie scored three "Drabs" for donning a neon wig and no top under her blazer. Her one supporter was Natalie, who said she'd rock that ensemble in a heartbeat!
