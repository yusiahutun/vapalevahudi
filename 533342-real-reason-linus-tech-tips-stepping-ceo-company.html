Real reason Linus Tech Tips is stepping down as CEO of his company

The Canadian YouTuber shook the world when he announced that he was stepping down as the CEO of the companies he helped create and had been running for the past decade.

What has happened to Linus Tech Tips? On 18th May 2023, he announced that he would be stepping down as the Chief Executive Officer of his company, Linus Media Group. Even though the company is famous for running the Linus Tech Tips show, it also runs other companies, including:

  • Floatplane Media, Inc.
  • Creator Warehouse, Inc.
  • LTT Labs
  • Several podcasts and YouTube channels

Why did Linus Sebastian step down?

The acclaimed YouTube content creator stepped down as the CEO of Linus Media Group due to his lack of attention to detail and temperament required in effectively managing a firm with more than 100 workers.
