Normal People's Paul Mescal reveals hilariously embarrassing moment when filming those scenes

The actor chatted to James Corden about filming those intimate scenes

    Normal People stars Daisy Edgar-Jones and Paul Mescal have opened up about the hilarious side of filming intimate scenes on the hugely popular BBC Three show. Chatting to James Corden on the via FaceTime, Paul admitted that while the crew were hugely respectful of them both during filming, they didn't quite know what to do after they thought that either Daisy or Paul had farted during a take. 

    WATCH: Paul talks filming sex scene with Daisy

    The 24-year-old explained: "There was a day where we got covered in this fake sweat stuff and there was a scene where me and Daisy had to flip positions but, because our bodies were in close proximity when we turned it made this massive fart noise like, aggressively loud! Me and Daisy started cackling but the funniest part was that the crew were being so diligent that they were like, 'Okay, Paul or Daisy have farted here but we're not going to [make a thing of it].' So we had to tell them it was our bodies making contact."
