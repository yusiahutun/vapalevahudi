Kate Middleton Photographed in Casual-Chic Shopping Look

  • The Duchess of Cambridge was photographed while embarking on a casual shopping run not far from Kensington Palace yesterday.
  • Kate wore a baby blue Mulberry coat, matching turtleneck, skinny jeans, and a chic leather bag.
  • The shopping excursion follows a busy last week for the duchess, who recently completed a tour of Ireland, attended Commonwealth Day services, and hosted a charity gala back at Buckingham Palace.

Sometimes the Duchess of Cambridge just wants to keep it casual.

Duchess Kate was photographed out and about in London yesterday looking effortlessly fabulous on a shopping run. For the low-key errand, Kate donned a chic baby blue Mulberry overcoat, matching knit turtleneck, dark-wash skinny jeans, black leather loafers, and a cross-body bag. Kate wore her hair in her signature style—flowing waves—and accessorized with dainty hoop earrings.

XPOS//BACKGRID

According to the Daily Mail, the duchess was paying a visit to Waterstones, a bookstore not far from Kensington Palace, where she resides with Prince William and their children, Prince George, Princess Charlotte, and Prince Louis. Per the outlet, Kate was reportedly looking for "history and military-themed books for children"—presumably to take back home to her family.

Kate's shopping excursion was a rare moment of downtime for the royal. In the last week alone, she's completed a tour of Ireland alongside William, reunited with Prince Harry and Duchess Meghan for Commonwealth Day, and hosted a charity gala at Buckingham Palace.
