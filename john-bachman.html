John Bachman is an American TV personality who currently hosts the show 'Jon Bachman Now' at Newsmax TV. He previously worked at CBS 12 News WPEC and WRAL-TV.

John Bachman is Married! Learn about his Wife and Children

John Bachman is happily married to his college sweetheart Jennifer Kuzma Bachman for the past twenty-two years. The pair first met when they were students at St.Olaf College, Minnesota, and got married on October 2, 2009.

John Bachman Works at Newsmax TV; What is his Net Worth?

John Bachman, despite his father being from the journalism field, was not interested in broadcast initially. In fact, he wanted to be a doctor and joined a medical school. However, during college, he landed an internship at KELO-TV in Sioux Falls and got the chance to appear on TV. 

Bachman loved the experience, and when KELO-TV offered the job, he accepted. After that, he went to Raleigh, North Carolina, and later to WRAL-TV. After spending four years at the network, he moved to WBS-TV, Atlanta, and later to CBS 12 News WPEC.

John Bachman is the host of the show John Bachman Now at Newsmax TV. IMAGE SOURCE: Collier County Republican Party

The University of Georgia alum joined Newsmax in 2011 as a Digital News Editor. After more than two years in the post, he became the host and Managing Editor of the show John Bachman Now.

On average, a Newsmax Media anchor earns $84 851-$91,256 a year. But given, Bachman is a veteran journalist and also an editor; hence there is no doubt he is making a lot higher than the average salary. His Net Worth as of 2020 is estimated to be $700,000.
