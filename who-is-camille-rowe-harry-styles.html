Who is Camille Rowe? Facts about Harry Styles' girlfriend Camille Rowe

1

She's half-French

Camille Rowe-Pourcheresse was born to a French restaurant-owning father and an American model/dancer mother. She grew up between Ville-d'Avrey, New York and California.

2

She's already a big name in fashion

While the model may only have become a household name recently, she's actually been making a splash on the fashion scene for some time. Back in 2013, she starred alongside Robert Pattinson in this very sultry Dior advert. She's also fronted campaigns for the likes of Chloé, Tommy Hilfiger and H&M.

3

She appeared on the cover of Playboy

In April 2016, Rowe covered the US edition of Playboy – and shared the news on Instagram in her usual good humour.

4

She's a Victoria's Secret model

Getty

After posing for the brand since back in 2014, Rowe was invited to join the catwalk extravaganza last December, taking to the catwalk in her Victoria's Secret Pink ensemble. With her cool French-girl status, she was a surprise addition to the line up, but she'll almost certainly be making a comeback next year.

5

Styles isn't her first musician boyfriend

Before her rumoured romance with Harry Styles, Rowe has also reportedly been linked to MGMT's Andrew Van Wyngarden, as well as singer Devendra Banhart and Cameron Avery.

6

She's impossibly chic

Getty

A French model that has effortless style? Okay, this is not the most surprising fact about Rowe we'll admit, but her street style is something to behold. From chic white shirts to statement designer pieces and slogan T-shirts, she's a great inspiration when it comes to off-duty dressing.

7

She didn't set out to be a model

...but sort of fell into it. Rowe – while studying at university – was scouted at a café in Paris. She decided to put her education on hold and pursue the opportunity.

8

She has acting aspirations

As well as her high-flying modelling career, Rowe also has her sights set on Hollywood, having already starred in French Film, One Day Will Come.

9

Her exercise regime is anything but boring

Rowe keeps in supermodel shape by ensuring her gym routine is as fun as possible. "I go for hikes and do Hatha yoga most mornings to make me feel sane," she told Elle UK. "I recently I went on a 3am jet-lagged Amazon shop and bought a trampoline, which I'm now on constantly. It's apparently good for the lymphatic system."

10

She has plenty of famous pals

Getty

Including Hailey Baldwin, Bella Hadid and Alexa Chung (who is said to have introduced her to Harry Styles).
