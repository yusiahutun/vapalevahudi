So You Think You Can Dance Judges Salaries 2024: Net Worth, Contract

Time to dance. The So You Think You Can Dance judges’ salaries must be bigger and better this time around. Fox is reviving the series with a new format and the dancers are front and center this season.

Judges for season 18 include Jojo Siwa, Alison Holker, and Maksim Chmerkovskiy. Per a press release, the show will take viewers behind the scenes of the contestants’ personal lives into the series. Previously, audiences only knew the contestants from their dance performances. Now, they will get the full picture of the behind-the-scenes dynamics as the camera delves into the cast’s “daily struggles, new relationships, personality clashes, and more.”

“So You Think You Can Dance is television’s preeminent dance competition, and we are thrilled to bring the beloved show back for an all-new season,” said Allison Wallach, Fox Entertainment’s president of unscripted programming. “Reuniting Cat, Nigel, Allison and Comfort and, welcoming Maksim into the SYTYCD family, is sure to make Season 18 bigger than ever for their legions of fans around the globe.”

So what are the So You Think You Can Dance judges’ salaries? Read more to see what we know.

  • Jojo Siwa

    Net Worth: $20 million

    Jojo Siwa’s net worth is $20 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. She rose to fame on Dance Moms and became viral on Youtube. In 2019,  she made an estimated $27 million in revenue from her sold out D.R.E.A.M Tour.

    She talked about her early business strategy to Forbes, “Early on, Nickelodeon wanted to have a meeting, where they discussed big business, and they wanted to do it without me. My Mom and I said, ‘That’s not how it works. We’ve been in this together since day one.’ And in that meeting Pam, who is the head of consumer products, said, ‘Just so you know, if this t shirt doesn’t sell then it’s all over. That was why I didn’t want you here.’ Now, every time I see her I laugh and say, ‘How’d that t-shirt sell?’” 

  • Alison Holker

    Net Worth: $6 million
    Season: 18

    Allison Holker’s net worth is worth $6 million according to Celebrity Net Worth. She competed in season 4 and is returning to season 18 as a judge.

    Holder filed for control of half of their joint estate in February 2023 after her husband Stephen “tWitch” Boss died without a will. In court documents obtained by People, Holker filed a a California Spousal Property Petition in the Superior Court of California in Los Angeles County, which is standard procedure when spouses die without a will. According to People, though California law states that a surviving spouse is entitled to half of their deceased spouse’s joint estate once they’re dead, the living spouse still has to file the petition to prove they are actually the spouse of the person who died. Holker asked for “determination of property passing to the surviving spouse” as well as “confirmation of property belonging to the surviving spouse.” The court documents detailed property, including quasi-community property, that Holker asked the court to rule she will receive as tWitch’s surviving spouse. “This includes any interest in a trade or business name of any unincorporated business or an interest in any unincorporated business that the deceased partner was operating or managing at the time of death,” court documents state.

    Holker did not request for administration over tWitch’s estate. In the court documents, Holker alsoreported that tWitch had a “nil” net worth and that he didn’t receive any property through a gift, joint tenancy, life insurance or other beneficiaries after they married on December 10, 2023. The court documents also confirmed that tWitch lived in California throughout his whole marriage to Holker and that they didn’t have any written agreements between them on how their properties would be divided if one of them died. Along with half of their joint estate, Holker also requested half of tWitch’s Stephen Boss Productions company and Goldman Sachs investment account, as well as his royalties from Cast and Crew Production Services; Disney Worldwide Services, Inc.; GEP Talent Services, LLC; and SAG/AFTRA, according to the court documents.

  • Nigel Lythgoe

    Net Worth: $150 million
    Seasons: 1-17

    Nigel Lythegoe has a net worth of $150 million according to Celebrity Net Worth. In the meantime, I am dedicating myself to clearing my name and restoring my reputation.”
