Emma Stone layers a trench coat over a trench dress

Emma Stone brought her effortlessly cool style to the New York City Film Festival yesterday.

The actor walked the carpet at Lincoln Center’s Alice Tully Hall on Wednesday to attend the premiere of Bleat, an unconventional, surrealist short film by the Greek filmmaker Yorgos Lanthimos.

Her supremely autumnal outfit was made up of two layered trenches — a cropped beige trench coat layered over a sleeveless double-breasted trench minidress. Underneath, she wore sheer black tights and finished the look with pointed black leather heels with twisted bows at the front. The full look hails from Louis Vuitton's spring/summer 2024 collection.

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

The La La Land Oscar-winner accessorised simply, with a thin gold chain necklace and tiny gold earrings (also from the French fashion house). She opted for low-key make-up, with an emphasis on glowing skin and a glossy raspberry lip. Her hair was styled in a loose, wavy bob.

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

Stone stars in the silent, black-and-white Bleat, which tells the story of a beloved man’s death, and in which reality and life’s desires blend with imagination.

Stone and Lanthimos also worked together on this year’s Poor Things, a dark, steampunk rom-com that debuted at Venice Film Festival and has received outstanding reviews.

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

At yesterday’s premiere, Stone took the stage with the director. “Go SAG!” she cheered. The two were able to openly discuss Bleat, as the short film secured an interim agreement amid the ongoing SAG-AFTRA strike, which has paused most activity in Hollywood.

The New York Film Festival kicked off on September 29 and wraps up on October 15.
