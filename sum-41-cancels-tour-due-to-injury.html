Sum 41 Cancels Tour Due To Injury

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Canadian punk band Sum 41 has been forced to put the remainder of their tour on hiatus after frontman Deryck Whibley recovers from a herniated disk in his lower back.

The band had been touring with Finger Eleven, another Canadian band but have scrubbed dates at least until Nov. 8th as Whibley has been instructed by doctors to avoid performing for at least six weeks while he recovers, CanWest News Service reported. The tour was in support of their most recent release, "Underclass Hero" which has been getting positive notice from the Billboard charts.

Whibley is reportedly resting at his Los Angeles home where he resides with his wife Avril Lavigne. – CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
