Sophie Turner rocks a Playboy shirt while picking up groceries with husband Joe Jonas in Los Angeles

They've been keeping a low profile in recent months.

But Sophie Turner didn't look reserved at all when she stepped out in Los Angeles with her husband Joe Jonas, 32, on Thursday.

The 25-year-old Game Of Thrones star had on a playful powder powder blue Playboy sweatshirt as the couple headed to a grocery store after grabbing lunch together. 

Running errands: Sophie Turner, 25, and Joe Jonas, 32, stopped by a grocery store in LA on Thursday after grabbing lunch in the city

The couple were spotted exiting a Gelson's grocery store with several bags of supplies and some paper products.

Sophie complemented her casual shirt with a matching fuzzy blue hat, and she wore baggy white pants with slits running up to her ankles.

She stayed on theme with pale blue and white sneakers, and she masked up as they entered the grocery store in accordance with California's indoor mask mandate. 

Joe also looked casual with a plain black shirt, gray trousers and high stop sneakers, along with shaggy hair.

He had on a green jacket with 'Marty' embroidered over the breast pocket. 

No fuss: She looked playful in a blue Playboy sweatshirt and a matching fuzzy hat, while Joe had on a green jacket with gray trousers and black high tops

The jacket appears to be either part of his costume or a gift from the production of his upcoming war film Devotion.

Joe plays a supporting role in the Korean War–set film about two fighter pilots, played by Lovecraft Country's Jonathan Majors and Top Gun: Maverick's Glen Powell.

He appears to have already completed filming, though a release date hasn't been set for the picture.

It's the Camp Rock star's second feature film ever, following his small role in Zoolander 2.

On point: Sophie also had on baggy white pants and matched her blue shoes with her shirt

New venture: Joe is following in his brother Nick's footsteps by focusing on movies. He stars in the upcoming Korean War drama Devotion, which his jacket was from; seen December 10 in NYC

Sophie is also ready to get back to acting after her pace slowed early on in the ongoing pandemic.

She's set to appear in the upcoming miniseries The Staircase, based on the crime docuseries of the same name.

The show stars Colin Firth as Michael Peterson, who was accused of killing his wife Kathleen after she was found dead at the bottom of a staircase in their home.

Sophie is playing his adopted daughter Margaret Ratliff, who stands by her father throughout his long trial.

Michael Stuhlbarg, Rosemarie DeWitt, Dane DeHaan, Toni Collette and Patrick Schwarzenegger are also featured in the ensemble series. 

Last year, she voiced Princess Charlotte on the animated comedy The Prince, and she gave her husband a hard time on the Jonas Brothers Family Roast for Netflix.

Not holding back! Late last year, Sophie raunchily poked fun at Joe for his old purity ring — which he wore despite 'sticking his fingers in costars' — for Netflix's Jonas Brothers Family Roast

In the hilarious special, she poked fun at Joe and his famous brothers for their 'toe curlingly lame' purity rings, which don't seem to have done much to maintain Joe's purity early in his career.

The actress explained how all three brothers had the rings to demonstrate that they were supposedly committed to abstaining from sex until marriage. 

But instead of just singling out Joe, she lambasted the entire concept.

'I know this is just a roast, and you all think I should be going after them, but I think we need to set the record straight here — no, the rings weren't a good idea. Yes, as a gesture, they're laughably, toe curlingly lame,' she said. 

Burn! Sophie called the Jonas Brothers' purity rings 'toe curlingly lame' and joked that Joe's earlier dalliances were 'like Cinderella because most of the girls he tried it with were under contract to Disney'

'But remember, this was about more than a gesture,' she continued. 'This was about faith. This was about principal. this was about taking a stand and setting an example.' 

Unfortunately her future love didn't abide by his own principles.

'Look, Joe Jonas wasn't just sticking his fingers in some dumb metal rings. He was sticking his fingers in costars, actresses and even a supermodel or two,' she said, as Priyanka doubled over with laughter. 

She connected Joe's deception to their eventual romance via a classic fairy tale.

'To be honest, Joe and the purity ring thing was kind of like a modern day Cinderella,” Sophie said. 'I mean, Joe tried to find a match for his purity rings a few times, but finally, the finger fit me. It was also like Cinderella because most of the girls he tried it with were under contract to Disney.'
