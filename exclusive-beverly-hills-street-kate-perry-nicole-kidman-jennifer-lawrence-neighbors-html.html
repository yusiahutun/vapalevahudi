Exclusive Beverly Hills street where Kate Perry, Nicole Kidman and Jennifer Lawrence are neighbors

Tucked away in Beverly Hills lies one of the world's most glamorous communities where Hollywood and music's most famous faces live side-by-side in a high-security and secretive area.

The celebrity neighborhood sits in a heavily-fortified and privately-patrolled area known as 'Hidden Valley'.

Out of 33 properties, 11 are owned by mega-stars such as Adele, Katy Perry, Jennifer Lawrence, Nicole Kidman and Keith Urban, Cameron Diaz, Ashton Kutcher, Nicole Richie and Penelope Cruz. 

Cruz lives next door to Adele, who also lives beside Richie, who's opposite Avatar actress Zoe Saldana, the next-door neighbor of Lawrence, just a stone's throw away from Kutcher.

Adele and Perry both love it so much that they've spent nearly $50 million on four properties.

Three years ago, the British singer purchased a four-bedroom, six-bathroom home for $9.5 million, complete with pool and summerhouse, directly across the road from her best friend Lawrence, 28. 

A month after splitting up from husband Simon Konecki, 45, in April, Adele, 31, bought the six-bed property opposite for $10.65 million.

Friend Jennifer Lawrence purchased her five-bed property from Jessica Simpson for $8.2 million in October 2014, which was also owned by Ellen DeGenres and Australian actor Paul Hogan.

Hidden Valley shouldn't be confused with the gated community a few miles away in Calabasas called 'Hidden Hills', which houses most of the Kardashian and Jenner family. 

But even that can't compete with the sheer number of celebrities within this 200-meter-squared space.
