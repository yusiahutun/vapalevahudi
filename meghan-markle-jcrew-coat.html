Meghan Markle Wore a J.Crew Coat and Slouchy Beanie

Over the weekend, Meghan Markle and Prince Harry were spotted outside of their natural habitat of Santa Barbara and got snapped running errands in Beverly Hills. According to Page Six, Meghan was wearing J.Crew's Chateau Parka, which retails for $375 and is still available in an array of colors and sizes (Meghan chose Heather Dark Walnut for anyone wanting to bundle up like a Sussex). She wore the faux-fur lined coat with knee-high boots, skinny jeans, and a black top. The finishing touches included the requisite L.A.-approved slouchy beanie (specifically the Cashmere Slouchy Cuff Beanie from Hat Attack) and oversized sunglasses.

Instagram account @harry_meghan_updates posted shots of the look and included Prince Harry's outfit, which seemed to belie the chill that Meghan was staving off with her wool coat.
