Real Housewife Yolanda Foster debuts new bob hairstyle for 50th birthday

Real Housewife Yolanda Foster debuts new bob hairstyle for her 50th birthday - as daughter's throwback bikini photo shows she hasn't aged in 15 years

Yolanda Foster celebrated her 50th birthday on January 11 with a sleek new bob haircut.

The real Housewives of Beverly Hills cast member debuted her new cropped style on Instagram, telling her followers how she is ‘excited about starting a new decade.'

Mrs Foster's birthday was also marked by additional photos which reveal her love for her husband, and also how she has not aged in the last 15 years.

Birthday look: Yolanda Foster, a cast member of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, has debuted a new chiseled bob for her 50th birthday

Former look: Mrs Foster previously wore her hair in a longer style and would often pull it back into a tight ponytail

The reality star posted a photo with her husband, famous music producer David Foster, to her Instagram in which she writes ‘My life, my love, my happiness #blessed.’

A ‘throw back Thursday’ Instagram posted by her 17-year-old daughter Bella Hadid (whose father is Mohamed Hadid, Mrs Foster’s first husband), shows how little Mrs Foster has aged in the last couple of decades.

Posing with her three children on a horse, Ms Foster appears just as lithe, blonde, and fit as she does this very day.

With her husband: Mrs Foster also posted a birthday photo with her husband - the acclaimed music producer David Foster (left)

Of the photo Ms Hadid wrote: ‘Happy birthday to the strongest, kindest, most beautiful woman in the world. Thank you for being the best mother I have ever known. You are my angel and best friend and I will love you endlessly forever & ever @yolandahfoster.'

Mrs Foster married her current husband in 2011, and raises her three children as well as five stepchildren.

In addition to Bella, Mrs Foster also has an older daughter named Gigi (age 18), and a younger son named Anwar (age 14).

Forever young: A 'throw back Thursday' Instagram posted by Mrs Foster's daughter Bella Hadid shows how little her mother has aged

Modern day: Mrs Foster looks remarkably the same in a recent photo with her three children and husband, David Foster

Prior to her big birthday, Mrs Foster - a former model who originally hails from The Netherlands - had formerly sported a long hairstyle that she would often pull back into a tight ponytail.

She moved to LA in the mid-Nineties after culminating an international modelling career that took her to places as diverse as Tokyo and Hamburg.

Mrs Foster now operates ‘Hopelessly Romantic’ – a company that helps couples "get wise and keep their romance alive!
