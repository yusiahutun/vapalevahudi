Seongnam FC was founded in 1989.

Established in 1989, Seongnam FC is a professional football club based in Seongnam, South Korea. The club has a rich history and has become one of the most successful football clubs in the country.

The club is nicknamed “The Magpies”.

Seongnam FC is commonly known as “The Magpies” due to their black and white striped home kit, resembling the appearance of the magpie bird.

Seongnam FC has won multiple K League titles.

The club has a strong track record of success, having won the K League title a total of seven times. This includes four consecutive titles from 1993 to 1996.

Seongnam FC has also achieved success in continental competitions.

The club has had success in the Asian Football Confederation (AFC) Champions League, winning the title three times in 1995, 2000, and 2010.

Park Joo-ho, a South Korean international, played for Seongnam FC before making a name for himself in European football, notably with clubs like Borussia Dortmund and Ulsan Hyundai.

Seongnam FC has a passionate fanbase.

The club’s home stadium is Tancheon Sports Complex.

Seongnam FC plays their home matches at the Tancheon Sports Complex, a modern stadium with a seating capacity of around 16,800 spectators.

Seongnam FC places a heavy emphasis on youth development and has a well-established youth academy that nurtures young talents and prepares them for professional football.
