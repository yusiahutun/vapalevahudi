Moonstruck is a romantic comedy film that follows the love life of Loretta Castorini, played by Cher. She is a sensible woman who agrees to marry her boyfriend, Johnny, when he proposes. However, while Johnny visits his dying mother in Sicily, Loretta falls for his younger brother Ronny.

As their unexpected romance unfolds, Loretta must navigate her complicated feelings and deal with her overbearing Italian-American family, who also face their romantic entanglements.

1. Cher

Cher, born Cherilyn Sarkisian on 20 May 1946 in El Centro, California, United States, played Loretta Castorini. Loretta Castorini is a bookkeeper from Brooklyn, New York, who finds herself in a complicated situation when she falls for the brother of the man she has agreed to marry.

Cher is still alive as of 2024. In October 2023, she released the song DJ Play A Christmas Song and appeared in the TV special Carol Burnett: 90 Years of Laughter + Love.

How old was Cher when she shot Moonstruck? Cher was 41 years old when she shot Moonstruck.

2. Nicolas Cage

Nicolas Cage was born Nicolas Kim Coppola on 7 January 1964 (age 60 as of 2024) in Long Beach, California, United States. He played Ronny Cammareri, a baker and the estranged younger brother of Johnny Cammareri, who is engaged to Loretta Castorini.

3. Olympia Dukakis as Rose Castorini

Olympia Dukakis played Rose Castorini, Loretta Castorini's mother, who provided wisdom and guidance to her daughter. Unfortunately, Olympia Dukakis is one of the Moonstruck cast members who are dead.

Olympia Dukakis died on 1 May 2021 at the age of 89. Her last film project was playing a Judge in Not to Forget (2021), which was released posthumously.

Vincent Gardenia played Cosmo Castorini, the father of Loretta Castorini, a successful plumber, and the husband of Rose Castorini. According to the Washington Post, Vincent Gardenia died on 9 December 1992 at 72. His last film project was in 1991, when he played Big Lou Kritski in The Super.

5. Danny Aiello

Danny Aiello played Johnny Cammareri. Johnny Cammareri is the fiancé of Loretta Castorini and is depicted as an overgrown mama boy who appeals to Loretta's common sense.

Unfortunately, Danny died on 12 December 2019 at 86. His last film project was playing Joe McGuinness in One Moment, released posthumously in 2021.

6. Julie Bovasso

Julia Bovasso played Rita Cappomagi or Aunt Rita, the aunt of Loretta Castorini, in Moonstruck and remains one of the most memorable characters in the film. 7. Louis Guss

Louis Guss played Raymond Cappomagi in Moonstruck. Raymond Cappomagi is Rita Cappomagi's husband and Loretta Castorini's uncle. Unfortunately, Louis Guss died on 29 September 2008 at 90. His last film project was playing a Court Clerk in Find Me Guilty (2006).

John Mahoney played Perry in Moonstruck. Perry is a college professor in a dramatic situation when a female student unceremoniously dumps him.

9. Feodor Chaliapin Jr.

Feodor Chaliapin played Grandpa Castorini in Moonstruck. He is the grandfather of Loretta Castorini. Unfortunately, he died on 17 September 1992 at the age of 86. His last project was playing Sam Marburg in the French film Max et Jérémie.

Anita Gillette played Mona in Moonstruck. Mona is the mistress of Cosmo Castorini, the father of Loretta Castorini. As of 2024, Anita is still alive. She was born on 16 August 1936 (age 88) in Baltimore, Maryland, United States.
