EastEnders original Dotty Cotton star selling OnlyFans pics

EastEnders bombshell Molly Conlin, who played the original Dotty Cotton, has sent her fans wild with a huge announcement that she is joining racy website OnlyFans to sell steamy snaps.

Molly, 21, played evil Dotty from 2008 before her exit in 2010, after she was exposed for plotting against her grandmother Dot with her dad Nick.

Dotty later returned to the soap in 2019 but was played by Milly Zero, who remains on the cast to this day.

Despite sharing her upset at being replaced at the time, Molly has gone from strength to strength as she announced she is joining OnlyFans.

Taking to her Instagram Story, Molly shared a screenshot of her OnlyFans profile to announce to her fans that she was on the site so they could purchase her content.

Molly is now selling pictures for a subscription at £7.60 a month or £15 for three months, and has already shared 28 posts.

This comes after Molly's latest racy snaps on Instagram, as she sizzled in a daring cut-out dress held together by string in a seriously sultry display.

Molly appeared to ditch her underwear as she flashed side-cleavage in the asymmetrical black dress, showing off her curves as she posed in front of a full length mirror.

The brunette bombshell smouldered for the camera as she paraded her toned figure in the skimpy dress, which featured a short hemline that finished just under her peachy derriere.

Molly let her dark hair tumble around her shoulders into glamorous waves as she also showed off dramatic make-up, opting for long lashes and a slick of lipstick on her pout.

It didn't take long for her fans to rush to the picture to compliment her on the jaw-dropping display, as one posted: "Wow unbelievable stunner."

Another added: "Absolutely gorgeous," while a third said: "Unreal."
