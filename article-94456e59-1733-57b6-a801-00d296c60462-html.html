West Jones football season tickets on sale now | Sports

Reserved-seat season tickets for the 2017 West Jones football season are currently on sale at the WJHS main office through July 28.

Tickets will be sold at the office from 7:30 a.m. until 3 p.m. each day during the normal work week. Tickets are $48 each for the six home games featured on the Mustangs’ schedule.
