Emily Ratajkowski Makes a Fall Statement in Cow-Print Boots

Emily Ratajkowski’s fall wardrobe is all about everyday staples and bold statement pieces. The model has been showing off her fabulous coat closet all over New York City this season, and for her latest coat-centric look, she added another eye-catching accessory: cow-print boots.

This time around, the coat of the day was long matte leather in an olive green, lined with a cream-colored shearling lining. The piece featured a collar—also coated in shearling—and cozy pockets. EmRata wore it with a beige knit sweater, gray jeans, and knee-high brown-and-tan cow-print calfskin boots with black block heels. (A similar style is available at Schutz.)

The My Body author further accessorized with large black sunglasses and big gold hoop earrings. For glam, she wore an easy straight hairdo and a glossy lip.

Ignat/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Ratajkowski’s latest coat look comes after she showed out in New York wearing two different burnt-orange coats in one week—one a glossy belted leather trench, and the other a puffier orange belted coat with a vintage texture. She has also been rocking one black leather trench after the other, often finishing the ensembles with knee-high boots or her go-to sneakers. And earlier this month, EmRata debuted one of her most striking fall looks yet when she paired a long caramel coat with creamy shearling lining with an ultra-cropped black top from Mirror Palais, straight-leg khakis cinched with a big green belt, and black sneakers.
