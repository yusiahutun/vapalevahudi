Ryan Reynolds' record Wrexham signing which was KO'd agrees deal with new club

Ryan Reynolds' Wrexham have been dealt a huge blow ahead of the January transfer window.

Back in the summer, Wrexham had been minutes away from securing a deal with Harrogate Town striker Luke Armstrong - but the paperwork wasn't completed before the 11pm deadline. Instead, Armstrong remained in North Yorkshire, where he has continued to impress.

However, Reynolds and co won't be able to move again for Armstrong, who looks set to leave Harrogate in two weeks. Armstrong has put pen-to-paper on a deal with Carlisle, making him their first signing under their new owners.
