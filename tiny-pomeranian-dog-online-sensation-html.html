Tiny Pomeranian dog becomes an online sensation

Being a pet owner can have lots of positive aspects. Pets can be our best friends, confidants, motivators and even our teachers. 

That's what Yhohan Kim from South Korea thought when he owned his tiny Pomeranian puppy named Snowball. But one thing that he could not imagine is his dog becoming an internet sensation. 

There is no doubt that the very playful white Pom-pom loves to be the centre of attention. In the beginning it appears to be shy, but the more time passes, its fun loving personality comes out.

Pomeranians like Snowball are considered loyal companions who are highly protective of their owners. They make the perfect choice for anyone who is looking for a small dog to serve as a faithful friend.
