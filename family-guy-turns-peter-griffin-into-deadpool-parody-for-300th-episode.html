'Family Guy' Turns Peter Griffin Into Deadpool Parody for 300th Episode

"Family Guy" hit a major milestone on Sunday by airing its 300th episode, and to celebrate they brought on the world's most famous superhero: Red Stool?

In a parody of Marvel Comics' Deadpool (but let's be honest, he belongs to Ryan Reynolds now, doesn't he?), Peter Griffin underwent a secret treatment to give him super powers, much like the Weapon X program turned Wade Wells into Deadpool.

Unfortunately, things didn't go quite as smoothly as Peter might have liked, and he emerged as Red Stool, instead. "The treatment didn't give me powers, but it did spread cancer to my anus," Peter explained directly to the camera before abruptly having to fight a horde of ninjas ... in slow motion ... as you do.

In a further nod to the film, "Family Guy" offered a new twist on Juice Newton's "Angel of the Morning" for the scene, now rewritten as "Anus of the Morning" and featuring great lines like, "This violence juxtaposed to music, anus" and "decapitation in slow motion, anus."
