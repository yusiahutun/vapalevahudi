Maisie Williams at NSPCC Summer Masquerade Ball

She's best known for her fierce and feisty role as Arya Stark in Game Of Thrones.

But Maisie Williams took a break from dragon-slaying on Saturday, to show off her more glamorous side at the NSPCC Summer Masquerade Ball in Bath, England. 

The 19-year-old actress looked red carpet ready as she attended the black tie event in a daring monochrome-themed dress.

Smile! Braless Maisie Williams looked red carpet ready as she attended the NSPCC Summer Masquerade Ball in Bath, England.  in a daring white lace dress 

The floor-length white lace dress featured layered ruffles and short sleeves, cinched in at the waist with a slim black belt.

The English actress looked ethereal as she opted for the delicate lace number, teamed with a delicate black belt and white sandals.

In keeping with the colour code, Maisie wore a black lace fascinator and showed off her gothic black manicure and pedicure in white strappy sandals.

Highlighting her trademark bold eyebrows, the award-winning teen actress wore a nude lip, tying her raven hair back in a loose ponytail.

Completing the look, she accessorised with gold drop earrings and a matching bracelet. 

The teen also posed alongside Martin Roberts from Homes under the Hammer and Imogen Sellers for Points West.

The stars were attending an auction to celebrate 30 years of Childline, held at Bath’s prestigious venue The Assembly Rooms. 

Maisie made her professional acting debut as Arya in the HBO fantasy television series Game Of Thrones, for which she won the EWwy Award for Best Supporting Actress in a Drama, the Portal Award for Best Supporting Actress and the Saturn Award for Best Performance by a Younger Actor.

Additionally she made her feature film debut in the mystery The Falling, for which she won the London Film Critics' Circle Award for Young Performer of the Year.
