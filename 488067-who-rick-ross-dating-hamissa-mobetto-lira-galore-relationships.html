Who is Rick Ross?

William Leonard Roberts II, known among his fans as Rick Ross, was born on 28 January 1976 in Clarksdale, Mississippi, United States. His parents are Tommie and William Leonard Roberts Sr. His family relocated to Miami, Florida, where the American rapper was raised alongside his sister Tawanda Roberts.

Rick Ross' debut album Port of Miami, was released in August 2006. The album reached #1 on Billboard Hot 200 with sales at 187,000 units during the first week. He has since released several albums, including Trilla, Deeper Than Rap, Mastermind and Richer Than I Ever Been. Ross worked as a correctional officer before making it big in the music industry.
