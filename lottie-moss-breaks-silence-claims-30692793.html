Lottie Moss breaks silence on claims she slept with Spencer Matthews at wedding

Lottie Moss has opened up on allegations that she slept with Made in Chelsea star Spencer Matthews.

The former model and Celebs Go Dating contestant was made to speak out after clips emerged on Tiktok that claimed she hooked up with Matthews, 35.

The videos racked up more than 111 million views, prompting the OnlyFans star to respond.

Speaking to Fabulous magazine, 25-year-old Lottie denied sleeping with Spencer, who is married to Irish TV personality Vogue Williams.

"Spencer's a great guy. We partied together but there was nothing more to it."

Allegations that Lottie had been a home-wrecker also reportedly spread like wildfire on WhatsApp.

The speculated hookup was said to have taken place at pal Jamie Laing's wedding to Sophie Habboo in Marbella in May.

The event was attended by a plethora of A-list celebrities, including professional dancer Oti Mabuse alongside other Made in Chelsea co-stars.

During the nuptials, Spencer had an important role, as he was the master of ceremonies.

Matthews had attended the event alone after a scheduling conflict for Vogue meant she missed out on the happy occasion.

Addressing the rumours, Kate Moss' sister Lottie completely refuted the claims: "Oh my God. It's not true.

"It didn't happen. Why would I bother addressing something that has no truth to it? Everyone at that wedding knows it didn't happen."

Moss said during the event, she had been with fellow Made in Chelsea star Liv Bentley.

She admitted that the rumours had gotten "crazy", to the point that Liv had been linked with Spencer too.

"I think it was because it was such a big wedding and it was full of all the reality stars, so people wanted some gossip."

Lottie said she wanted to clear the air to try and stem the rumour and stop people quizzing her on the subject, after users on Reddit and Tattle Life added fuel to the fire.

Moss has been pals with Matthews for years, and both share similar experiences with alcohol, having both overcome their own drinking struggles.

Lottie opted to ditch booze after she was admitted to rehab in Arizona in last year, whilst Spencer would have lost wife Vogue if he did not give up the bottle.
