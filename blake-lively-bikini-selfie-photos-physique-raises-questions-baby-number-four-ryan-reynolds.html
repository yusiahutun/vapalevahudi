Blake Lively's new bikini photo raises questions as she shares family photos with Ryan Reynolds afte

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds are enjoying some well deserved downtime and have jetted off on a tropical vacation with their loved ones. 

The stars recently welcomed their forth child , but you wouldn't know it from Blake's bikini photos from her getaway. 

Blake took to Instagram with snapshots and she looked incredible posing in a bikini top and a cut-out one-piece too. 

One standout image saw Blake posing poolside in a black swimsuit and her social media followers couldn't help but ask, "Didn't she just have a baby?"

Others commented: "You look like you never had children…how do you do it?" and, "Guys she's had her 4th kid".It appeared the couple were joined by their moms on the trip which looked magical.
