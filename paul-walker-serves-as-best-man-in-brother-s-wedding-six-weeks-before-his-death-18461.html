Paul Walker Serves As Best Man In Brothers Wedding Six Weeks Before His Death

Six weeks before his death in a horrific car crash, Paul Walker reportedly served as best man in his brother Caleb’s wedding, reports the New York Daily News.

Photos obtained by the paper show Paul, 40, and Caleb, 36, happily posing outdoors in their tuxes and in group shots with the rest of the wedding party. The wedding supposedly took place on Oct. 19 at Dove Canyon Country Club in Rancho Santa Margarita, Calif.

The past couple of days have understandably been rough for the Walker family, especially for Paul’s father, Paul Walker III, who broke down in tears while speaking to CBS2 about his son's death. “I'm just glad that every time I saw him, I told him I loved him," he said. "And he would say the same thing to me."

Walker Sr. also shared that he was “proud of [Paul] every day of his life.”
