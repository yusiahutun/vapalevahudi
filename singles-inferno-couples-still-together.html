Where Couples are Now 2024

Will they all make it out of Inferno? With the new season premiering, many Netflix dating show fans might be wondering who from Single’s Inferno is still together.

Single’s Inferno debuted in 2021 and skyrocketed to be one of Netflix’s most watched reality TV shows due to the shocking and dramatic nature. 12 contestants are put on a deserted island called “Inferno” and compete through challenges to couple up. Once a desired couple is matched, they are sent to “Paradise” which is a fancy luxury hotel where they could be able to get to know one another. Along with all the suspense and pairing, a set of panelists comment on the show including comedian Hong Jin-kyung, actress Lee Da-hee, Super Junior member Kyuhyun, rapper Hanhae, and YouTuber Dex.

In an interview with Netflix Tudum, producer Kim Na-hyun shared if there’s room for the cast to be in touch with each other after they leave the island. “I see them interacting on Instagram, so I think it’s safe to say that they’re still in contact. Because they spent eight nights and nine days on an island together, where they not only built relationships but also friendships, they are probably still close.”

So who from Single’s Inferno is still together? Read more to find out!

  • Song Ji-a & Kim Hyun-Joong

    Season: 1
    Status: Broken Up

    The stunning visual of season 1 Song Ji-A left with her “puppy” Kim Hyun-Joong at the end of season 1. However, the two split shortly after. Song Ji-a was criticized by fans for wearing fake designer clothes, and fans spotted that Hyun-joong unfollowed Ji-a on Instagram.

    Hyun-joong cleared up that the two remain amicable towards each other. “There had been a lot of attention [on us] and Ji A also wasn’t following me,” he told Maeil Business Newspaper via Koreaboo. “I didn’t want unnecessary rumors to start circulating so that’s why I decided to unfollow her too. There were no other reasons. Honestly, I had no idea that there was a controversy at the time.

    He continued, “Because of the ongoing situation, I had an even harder time reaching out to her. Even right now, in this moment as I talk about it, it’s really hard. However, what I can say is that the Ji-a I got to know is a kind and bright friend who works extremely hard.”

  • Kang So-yeon & Oh Jin-taek

    Season: 1

    Status: Broken Up

    Kang So-yeon and Oh Jin-taek looked like one of the most promising couples of Single’s Inferno season 1. However, things shortly ended after they left the island. Jin-taek is currently in a relationship with PR Agent Lily Baek.

  • Kim Jun-sik & An Yea-won

    Season: 1
    Status: Broken Up

    Kim Jun-sik and An Yea-won left the island hand in hand at the end of Single’s Inferno season 1. However, it all ended pretty quickly for them. They went on together on Cha Hyun Seung TV to talk about their experiences on the show and confirmed that they were just friends.

  • Moon Se-hoon & Shin Je-yoon

    Season: 1

    Status: Broken up

    Though they left the Island together at the end of season 1, Moon Se-hoon and Shin Je-yoon are just friends. Je-yoon invited Se-hoon to her vlog and they clarified that she’s still single.

  • Kim Se-Jun and Lee So-e

    Season: 2

    Status: Broken Up

    Kim Se-Jun and Lee So-e left together after appearing on season 2 of Single’s Inferno. After the show, Se-jun called Lee So-e his “dongsaeng” which means little sister in Korean. So, the two didn’t work out but still are great friends leaving cute Instagram comments on each other’s profile.

  • Jo Yoong-jae & Choi Seo-eun

    Season: 2
    Status: Dating

    Jo Yoong-jae and Choi Seo-eun are still dating after season 2 of Single’s Inferno. stockbroker Yoong-jae and artist Seo-eun instantly picked each other. When it came to the finale, Han-bin chose Seo-eun but she ultimately chose Yoong-jae. In an Instagram live, they were asked if they were going to get married and Yoong-jae responded, “I hope so.” 

  • Lee Gwan-hee and Choi Hye-seon

    Season: 3
    Status: Unknown, Likely Together

    Are Gwan-hee and Hye-seon still together after Single’s Inferno season 3? It’s unknown whether or not Gwan-hee and Hye-seon are still together after the show ended. Gwan-hee posted a photo of the cast members and some fans on Reddit noticed that Hye-seon posted a comment that translated to, “the pic is too dark, I told you if you want to post lmk first so I can add filter” and deleted it quickly.

    Gwan-hee was a source of drama in the show with the kinds of games he plays with his love interests, but he decided to settle down with Hye-seon after an emotional conversation during one of the last days on the island. I felt like I would keep thinking about Hye-seon if I picked Min-ji but I didn’t think I would think about Min-ji as much if I picked Hye-Seon. So in the end, I didn’t hesitate in picking Hye-Seon. “

  • Choi Min-woo and Yu Si-eun

    Season: 3
    Status: Unknown


    Are Si-eun and Min-woo still together after Single’s Inferno season 3? It’s unknown whether of not Si-eun and Min-woo are still officially together. However, fans noticed that Min-woo has been posting fan edits of the endgame couple on Instagram—leaving them to speculate that they’re still going strong.

    Min-woo and Si-eun made the panelists gush after their exchange at the end of their stay at Inferno, “You look like a princess.” Si-eun replied, “Then you’re my prince.”

  • Park Min-kyu and Kim Gyu-ri

    Season: 3
    Status: Unknown

    Are Gyu-ri & Min-kyu still together after Single’s Inferno season 3? It’s not known whether or not Gyu-ri and Min-kyu are still together as they haven’t posted pictures of each other on their Instagrams other than the cast photo. They also do not follow each other’s Instagram accounts.

    Gyu-ri picked Min-kyu after believing he was a steady man.  “I’ll get you plenty of blueberry pies once we’re out,” Min-kyu told her as they were leaving together. Gyu-ri asked, “How many?” Min-kyu said, “As many as you want. Until you can’t eat anymore.” 

  • Lee Jin-seok and An Min-young

    Season: 3
    Status: Unknown
    It’s unknown whether or not Jin-seok and Min-young are still together after leaving Inferno together during the finale.

    After the finale aired on Netflix, Jin-seok proudly posted a picture of himself at the cafe where he worked on his Instagram story. He also posted an Instagram post at a Single’s Inferno watch party with the cast with a photobooth picture of him standing firm with Ha-jeong. Meanwhile, Min-young has posted a couple of behind-the-scenes photos on her story along with Jin-seok.

    During the final moments of the last episode, Min-young said, “This is nerve-wracking. Oppa, thank you for taking such good care of me here. This wouldn’t have been an actual inferno if I didn’t have you for the ride” adding that she wishes him to bake her bread in his bakery.

  • Who are the Single's Inferno panelists?

    The Single’s Inferno panelists include comedian Hong Jin-kyung, actress Lee Da-hee, Super Junior member Kyuhyun, rapper Hanhae, and YouTuber Dex.

    Dex is a newcomer, having been a contestant on season 2 of Single’s Inferno. He told Netflix Tudum about his hopes for the new season. “I think the most important thing and what I’m looking forward to seeing the most is the level of genuine-ness the cast of Single’s Inferno 3 will bring. I hope we will feel like we are on the island alongside the cast and relate to the cast because of how well immersed they are on the island.”

    As for the advice he would give to the upcoming contestants, he said, “The most important thing would be to not lose yourself. I hope everyone is true to themselves, which is the most confident and cool way to appeal to their love interest rather than being someone you’re not. I wish everyone the best of luck!”

    Single’s Inferno is now streaming on Netflix.
