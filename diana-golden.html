About

Diana Golden is a 58 year old Mexican Actress. Born Diana Goldenberg Jiménez on 7th August, 1965 in Santiago de Cali, Valle del Cauca, Colombia, she is famous for her roles as Elvira Funes in the Televisa telenovela La Pícara Soñadora, Fabiola Mayerling San Román in the Televisa telenovela María Mercedes, and Amaranta, Viridiana, Eulalia and other characters in the Televisa anthology drama series Como Dice el Dicho.
