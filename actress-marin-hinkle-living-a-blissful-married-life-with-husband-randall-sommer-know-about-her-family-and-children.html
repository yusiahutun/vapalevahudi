Know more about Hinkle's husband Randall Sommer

Randall Sommer is no actor or director but he's a famous lawyer working for the huge law firm, Munger Tolles and Olson.

He currently works as a litigation partner in the Los Angeles office of Munger, Tolles & Olson.

He handles complex commercial cases, including class actions and contractual disputes, in the financial services, energy, retail, entertainment, and aerospace industries.

Sommer graduated from the University of California in Bachelor of Arts and later joined Columbia Law School practicing Doctor of Law.
