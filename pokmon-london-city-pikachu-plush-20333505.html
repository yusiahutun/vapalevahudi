Pokmon London City Pikachu plush toy revealed to mark launch of London Pokmon Center

The Pokémon Company have today announced an exclusive new toy that will only be available at their new pop up London Pokémon Center opening this October.

To mark the occasion, visitors will be able to purchase the exclusive London City Pikachu plush toy, dressed as though he is suited up to go to the office!.

This new London City Pikachu plush stands just under 20cm tall and cuts a dashing figure in his bowler hat, jacket and tie.

He’s also prepared for a rainy journey to the office with his trusty umbrella with its Psyduck inspired handle!

(place your bets on seeing this available exclusively in Pokemon Go at launch).

The special pop-up Pokémon Center will be located in Westfield London (Ariel Way, Shepherd’s Bush W12 7GF) and open only for a limited time from October 18 to November 15* 2019.

As you might imagine, the store will give fans a chance to browse and purchase a range of Pokémon Center-branded products, including exclusive designs not found anywhere else such as this London City Pikachu.

Fans will no doubt be aware, but the the pop-up Pokémon Center in London is being opened to celebrate the highly anticipated launch of the Pokémon Sword and Pokémon Shield video games for Nintendo Switch this November;.

The next big game in the series has a distinct design and feel that pays homage to the United Kingdom, which unfolds in the all-new Galar region.

Further details on opening times, products available to purchase and additional in-store activities will be revealed in the weeks.
