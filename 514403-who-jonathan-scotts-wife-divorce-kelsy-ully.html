Early life

The TV star was born Jonathan Silver Scott on 28 April 1978 (age 45 as of 2023) in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. He is the son of Joanne Scott (mother) and James Scott (father). He has an older sibling named JD Scott and a twin named Drew Scott.

Jonathan grew up in Maple Ridge, British Columbia. He attended Thomas Haney School, a public high school in Maple Ridge, British Columbia. After high school, he enrolled at the University of Calgary, public research university in Calgary, where he majored in business management.

He graduated from university, later pursuing further studies at Southern Alberta Institute of Technology and finishing his studies at Professional Home Builders Institute. Scott studied construction and design, enabling him to become a licensed contractor.

Professional career

After graduating from college, Jonathan Scott and his brother became real estate brokers. They purchased houses for small amounts and rented or sold them for profit. Although his real estate business was still intact, Scott became an illusionist.
