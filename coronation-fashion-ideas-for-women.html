Coronation fashion ideas for women: the most stylish red, white &amp; blue dresses

Fashion buys appropriate for a Coronation celebration...

    The Coronation of King Charles III will take place on Saturday 6 May at Westminster Abbey. Whether you're making the most of the bank holiday weekend with a street party, jetting abroad, or even heading to the royal residences in the hope of catching the 74-year-old monarch, you'll need to choose that all-important outfit.

    However you're celebrating, you can't go wrong wearing the patriotic colours of red, white and blue. From tonal styles to mixed hues in spring-ready prints, there are so many chic dresses on the high street right now, appropriate for everything from garden parties to more formal affairs.
