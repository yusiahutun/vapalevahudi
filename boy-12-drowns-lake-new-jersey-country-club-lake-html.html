Boy, 12, drowns in lake at New Jersey country club lake

A 12-year-old boy drowned in a lake at a high-end New Jersey country club on Wednesday, authorities have said.  

Police rushed to reports of a drowning at Lake Valhalla, which is a private members club in Montville which costs $6,000-a-year to join, according to the Morris County Prosecutor's Office.

The child, who has not been named, is understood to have been playing near a dock before he went underwater but never resurfaced.

A full membership to the plush club costs $6,000 for the initiation fee and a $1,500 annual membership fee, Patch reports.   

Police are investigating, but no foul play is suspected. The investigation will look into whether the club followed all the necessary rules.
