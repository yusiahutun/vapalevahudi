Drake Spoke Out After a Song Leaked Calling Kylie Jenner a &quot;Side Piece&quot;

Drake has issued a statement after on old song of his was leaked, with lyrics calling Kylie Jenner a "side piece."

In a video of Night Owl Sound's Instagram Live obtained by fans on Twitter, an unnamed collaboration between Drake and Future namedropped Jenner: "Real s—t, Kylie Jenner, that's a side piece / Yeah, I got 20 motherf—kin' Kylies."

The song also namechecked Kylie's sister Kendall and model Gigi Hadid: "Yeah, I got 20 damn Kendalls / Young slim baddies and they in Vogue / Yeah, I got 20 f—kin' Gigis."

After the leak went viral, Drake shared an Instagram story writing, "A song that Mark ran last night on Night Owl sound live set shouldn't have been played. It's a song that leaked 3 years ago and got scrapped shortly after. He was just going too deep in the drake/future catalogue. Last thing I'd want to do is wake up having any friends of mine feeling disrespected so I just had to stay that to start off the day."

Last year, rumors circulated that Drake and Kylie might have been dating after she and Travis Scott split up, though nothing was ever confirmed.

A source told People at the time thatJenner “could be [spending time with Drake] just to make [ex-boyfriend Travis Scott] jealous.” Otherwise, “Kylie doesn’t seem to be dating.”
