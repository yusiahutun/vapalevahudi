Perricone MD Acyl-Glutathione Review

It's rare to find a skincare product that will address the many signs of aging in one fell swoop. That's why we were excited to discover Perricone MD Acyl-Glutathione — a complexion treatment which combats major aging issues including severe dryness, extreme loss of elasticity, deep creases and lines, thinning skin, and enlarged pores. Although the formula is a moisturizing cream texture, the product isn't meant to replace your daytime serum or night cream — instead, apply it after cleansing and before moisturizing. It works by replenishing Glutathione levels — a naturally occurring antioxidant that rapidly depletes with age, stress, and environmental factors, which causes tired, dull, and dehydrated-looking skin. Studies show promising results in reduced facial lines and improved skin texture, elasticity and firmness after 30 days. Apply it morning and night.
