Elton John's mum leaves half her fortune to his former PA

Sir Elton John's mother left half of her £534,000 fortune to the former chauffeur and PA blamed for sparking their nine-year feud, it has been claimed.

Sheila Farebrother, who died aged 92 in December last year, reportedly left her son only two urns and some family photographs in her will.

But Elton's former assistant Bob Halley - whose departure from the star's entourage in 2008 triggered a family rift - was left half of all Sheila's wealth.

Sheila reportedly changed her will three weeks before dying on December 4.
