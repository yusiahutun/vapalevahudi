BBC Breakfasts Sally Nugent eyes up career U-turn as she auditions for new role

BBC Breakfast host Sally Nugent certainly raised some eyebrows after the brunette beauty “auditioned” for a new presenting gig live on the show.

During Tuesday’s visit (March 5) to the BBC studios, the 52-year-old welcomed viewers back to the morning programme alongside her co-host Jon Kay. The presenting duo discussed some of the biggest stories hitting the headlines.

This included the sale of Colin Firth's famous white shirt that he wore in the 1995 adaptation of Pride and Prejudice, the horrific treatment of homelessness in Manchester and the launch of the Celebrity Big Brother. Later on in the show the pair teased an upcoming interview with Kaiser Chiefs frontman Ricky Wilson.
