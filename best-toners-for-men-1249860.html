    How to Choose the Best Toner for Men?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before splurging on the best toner for men - 

    Skin Type: Always analyze your skin type before getting your hands on the best men’s toner. Please note, do not invest in any toner that is not meant for your skin type or condition. Scroll down to know what kind of men's facial toner you should vouch for.

    • For Dry Skin - Ensure that you pick a men's face toner that is loaded with hydrating and moisturizing ingredients like aloe vera, ceramides, natural oils, and rosewater.
    • For Oily Skin - It is wise to choose the best men's skin toner that is alcohol-free and crafted with exfoliating properties. Make sure that the toner contains AHAs, BHAs, and fruit enzymes that help in taking good care of your oily or acne-prone skin and controlling excess sebum production. 
    • For Normal And Combination Skin - Choose a toner that is free from harsh chemicals and additives. Stay away from toners that are infused with phthalates, sulfates, or parabens. 
    • For Sensitive Skin - Pick the best men's face toner that is gentle and crafted from mild herbal, natural, organic, and powerful ingredients like aloe vera, rosewater, and cucumber. Do not invest in toners that are formulated with synthetic fragrances as they might worsen your skin condition. 

    Ingredients: The best skin toner for men should contain pure, organic, skin-nourishing, and natural ingredients like aloe vera, rosewater, glycolic acid, bamboo extract, algae extract

    cucumber extracts, grape seed extract, lavender, green tea extract, and cica extract. In addition, it should be loaded with active ingredients like hyaluronic acid, salicylic acid, hydroxy acid, and alpha-hydroxy acid. These ingredients offer soothing benefits and help in cleansing, hydrating, and protecting your skin from harmful environmental stressors. Avoid choosing toners that contain sulfates, gluten, phthalates, silicones, and alcohol. 

    Formula: It is necessary to pay heed to the formula of the toner because that helps in predicting the efficacy of the product as a whole. Ensure that you splurge on any of the best men's toner that is lightweight, non-sticky, non-greasy, and primarily vegan and cruelty-free. 

    After taking note of the aspects mentioned above, roll your eyes over to acquaint yourself with the right steps for using one.

    When to Use a Toner?

    The following are the steps for using a toner accurately - 

    Step 1: Cleanse your face with a good-quality face wash or cleanser. 

    Step 2: Dab your face with gentle hands using a microfiber towel. 

    Step 3: Pick your toner, pour a sufficient quantity on a cotton ball or pad, and swipe it across your face. If the toner has a sprayer dispenser, hold it at a distance and spray. 

    Step 4: Hold on for a few seconds or until the toner dries completely.

    Step 5: Apply a moisturizer and follow it with sunscreen. 

    Tada, you are done!
