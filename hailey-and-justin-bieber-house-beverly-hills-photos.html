Hailey and Justin Bieber's fun $7.9million house they sold with all the furniture

The singer even asked his Instagram followers to buy his house

    Justin Bieber and his wife Hailey sold up their glorious home in Beverly Hills earlier this year – offloading it with all of the furniture. The singer splashed out a staggering $8.5million dollars (£6.9million) for it in 2019, but he posted about selling it just months later. They finally sold it and made a loss on the property when it closed for $7.9million this year. That didn't stop the star forking out $25.8million for another Beverly Hills home though. Take a look through the gallery to see Justin and Hailey's marital home which they used to live in…

    How dreamy is the bathroom? The spacious suite has a huge walk-in glass shower cubicle, with grey tiling on the walls, and bathmats from Justin's Drew House line.

    The living area is open plan and includes a cosy seating area with sofas and a television, a kitchen with a breakfast bar, and more seating in a bay window. The entire space is decorated in tonal shades of blue and white, with spotlights and statement pendants keeping the room bright.

    Another relaxing area had a huge portrait of Hailey and Justin on one wall, with a hanging chair in the corner. There are two hammered metal tables sat on fluffy rugs, with vases of fresh flowers and candles lining the wall.

    A set of stairs appear to lead down to a bar with a foosball table, slush puppy machines and racks of wine on display. Statement palm leaf wallpaper covers one wall, which also has a neon sign reading "The Tropics".

    The couple even had their own arcade games in their home, with a photo posted by Justin showing his "Super Shots" basketball game.

    The side of the kitchen wraps around to a huge open area with wooden flooring, a LEGO character on one wall, and several KAWS figures on display.

    Justin had more model figures on display in glass cases at the bottom of the stairs, which is also lined with an array of colourful skateboards.

    Hailey and Justin have previously shared a peek inside their bedroom, which had a huge television mounted on the wall opposite the bed, with a fireplace below and windows on either side. Much like the rest of the house, the room is white with wooden flooring, but fleecy throws, candles and lots of cushions help to create a cosy atmosphere.

    A previous post shared by Justin offered a glimpse towards the garden of the house, which has its own outdoor swimming pool and terrace lined by sun loungers and seating.
