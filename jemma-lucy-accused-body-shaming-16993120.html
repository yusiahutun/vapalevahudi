Jemma Lucy brands Chanelle Hayes 'fat' as spat gets seriously bitchy

The Ex On The Beach star sparked a feud with Big Brother star Chanelle over their respective bikini pictures.

Chanelle – who suffered a nip slip while holidaying in Alicante – saw a set of photos showing Jemma frolicking with BFF Chantelle Connelly in the Caribbean, but didn't seem too enamoured with the babes.

She tweeted: "Don't know who these 2 people are but they're always on Daily Mail...find them so gross, disgusting & embarrassing."

Jemma was soon alerted to the tweet – and didn't hold back.

She wrote: "Hahaha well now u do, here I am I'm Jemma Lucy. Nice to meet u u little trollop."

The tattooed bombshell then tweeted a picture of Chanelle wearing a bikini and tucking into a hot dog in a beachside café.

Her accompanying tweet read: "I'm embarrassing for bein on hols having a sick time? Think u need to put that hot dog down mate [sic]."

This tweet sparked accusations of body-shaming from Jemma's followers, who hit out at the star for insulting Chanelle's weight.

However, Jemma didn't seem to backtrack.

When a follower tweeted that Jem was body-shaming and wrote: "Telling her to put her food down and using a very visual photo of the fact she is a lot of curvier than what people would say 'slim' is", the reality star responded: "Not curvy. Fat. Let's not get s*** twisted. Those ain't curves."

Chanelle, who has struggled with yo-yo dieting, later addressed the spat, tweeting: "Can still be fat and successful without snogging my boyfriend's mum and getting horrific ink tattooed all over my face and body. Oh the youth of today…"

Jemma had the last word, later tweeting: "So Chanelle blocks us and still slags off my tattoos.. I'd rather have tatts than resemble a hippopotamus. Right I'm off to the beach."

We don't think anybody really won this battle – can't everyone just get along?
