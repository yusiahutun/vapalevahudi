Who is Norma Kuhling? Age and Career

Norma Kuhling was born on December 4, 1991, in New York with the birth sign Sagittarius. She was born to parents Michael Kuhling (father) and Kristi Zea (mother). As of 2019, she is 27 years old and belongs to American nationality.

She trained acting in 2012 from the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art and completed her course in 2014.

Talking about her early career, she debuted in the movie The Joneses playing a role as Beth in 2002. Later, in 2016, she played the movie Fallen as Rachel. Then, in 2017, she started to play the role of Dr. Ava Bekker on the television series Chicago Med. In the series, Norma appeared alongside actors, Nick Gehlfuss, Oliver Platt, Colin Donnell, Torrey DeVitto, among others.

Recently, in 2019, Actress Tallie Medel and Kuhling starred in NYC critic/filmmaker Dan Sallitt‘s fourth feature, Fourteen. The film premiered at Berlin International Film Festival and got an amazing critical response.
