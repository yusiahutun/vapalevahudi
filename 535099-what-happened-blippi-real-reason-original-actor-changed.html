Why did the original Blippi leave the show?

John, the original Blippi, left the show to spend more time and take care of his family. After teaching and entertaining children for over seven years, John and his fiancé were expecting one.

He shared the good news through his Instagram account on 27th October 2021. He shared a picture of him kissing his fiancé, Alyssa Ingham, who was holding a strip of ultrasound photos. The post was captioned:

I am going to be a dad. Alyssa and I are so excited. Parenthood coming 2022!

Who is the new Blippi?

Clayton Grimm is the new Blippi. He is an actor, musician, singer, and avid traveller. Even though many people consider Clayton a new character, he appeared in Blippi’s live tour in 2019. His appearance on the live show earned him the moniker 'Blippi from the live show'.

Yes. The original Blippi will be back on the show. According to the producer, Clayton Grimm was recruited to help Stevin John portray the title character, considering the massive expansion of the show.
