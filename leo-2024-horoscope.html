Leo 2024 Horoscope: Powerful Career Developments

You’re on the verge of a great awakening and your Leo 2024 horoscope says you’re starting—and ending—many important relationships this year. However, you’re also making major strides toward your career goals and landing opportunities you never could have predicted.

If you’ve been parting ways with people you never expected to leave behind, it’s partly because you got a taste of Pluto in Aquarius over the course of 2023. However, Pluto will re-enter Aquarius from January 20 to September 1, and again on November 19, activating your seventh house of allies, enemies, and significant others. This will attract intensity in your one-on-one relationships and transform your understanding of how a relationship should function. This year, the people you meet—and the ones you try to forget—will have lasting implications in your life over the course of the next 20 years.

However, 2024 will also be a highly spiritual year full of epiphanies and game-changing realizations. As the North Node of Destiny joins forces with Chiron—the wounded healer—in your ninth house of wisdom and expansion on February 19, you will begin to process the losses that have taken place and use them to learn more about the meaning of life. Not only are you rediscovering that knowledge is power, but you’re also learning how to hack your mindset and embrace a much more positive understanding. When a lunar eclipse in Libra lands in your third house of siblings, close friends, and daily communications as of March 25, a series of life-changing conversations could take place as your social circle begins to evolve and dissolve. And as a total solar eclipse casts a shadow over your ninth house of perspective on April 8, you may find yourself letting go of one set of beliefs in favor of another.

One of the most exciting moments for you in the course of 2024 arrives on April 20. This is when Jupiter will join forces with Uranus in your 10th house of publicity and career, activating a new source of inspiration in your professional pursuits. You may rediscover your goals from a new angle, break the internet with a meaningful press release, or simply take on the job of your dreams. Either way, you’re making waves in your field. And once Jupiter enters your 11th house of social outreach and global impact on May 25, you may find yourself meeting tons of new people for the rest of the year; people who will have so much to teach you, show you, and give you. It’s all about who you know!

By the time a lunar eclipse in Pisces sets off alarms in your eighth house of death and rebirth on September 17, you may uncover life-altering information that sparks a transformation. Secrets will be revealed, alliances will shift, and the level of trust you are able to give and receive will be challenged. This is soon to be followed by a solar eclipse in Libra on October 2, jumpstarting the engine in your third house of communication and and information, furthering your pursuit for knowledge and activating your desire to study.

However, as the year comes to a close, you may be forced to rethink the way you express yourself, judge yourself, and monitor your ego. As Mars stations retrograde in Leo as of December 6, it will challenge your will to win, which could punch the snooze button on your mojo. Luckily, this retrograde will also teach you how to let bygones be bygones, because you are worth so much more than these imaginary standards you’ve set for yourself.
