The Last Detail is a 1973 American comedy-drama film directed by Hal Ashby and written by Robert Towne. Starring Jack Nicholson, Randy Quaid, and Otis Young, the movie tells the story of two U.S. Navy men assigned to escort a young sailor to a naval prison, but along the journey, they find themselves questioning their own values and authority.

With its blend of comedy and drama, The Last Detail has become a cult classic and a true gem in American cinema. The film received critical acclaim upon its release and was nominated for three Academy Awards, showcasing the exceptional performances and compelling storytelling that captivated audiences worldwide.
