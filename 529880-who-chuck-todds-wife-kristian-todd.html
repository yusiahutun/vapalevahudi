Who is Chuck Todd?

Born on April 8, 1972, Chuck Todd is an American TV journalist who formerly served as NBC's Meet the Press 12th moderator. In addition, he was the host of NBC's Meet the Press Now and presently serves as the NBC News political director.

Before taking on the Meet the Press position, Todd held the pivotal role of NBC's chief White House correspondent and anchored MSNBC's The Daily Rundown. Furthermore, he is the political analyst for NBC's Today and NBC Nightly News. On September 17, 2023, Kristen Welker assumed the Meet the Press moderator role, succeeding Todd.
