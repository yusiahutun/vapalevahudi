Googlebox love triangle: Silent Jay heartbroken as ex Eve finds new love

The lad – who is famed for not uttering a word on the Channel 4 show – has been crushed by the news his ex has already found a new lover.

Eve has already started dating again after Heat uncovered that she’d updated her Facebook status to “In a relationship with Josh Smith”.

Things had already turned awkward between the pair after Jay was forced to go round to his ex’s house to film with her and mum Viv and step dad Ralph for Gogglebox.

Speaking of the split, Jay said last week he was hoping to win back Eve.

“It is such a shame. I wish we could work it out. I’m having to pretend to be happy families.

“The running joke is that I never say anything — so they say it won’t be a problem.”

Channel 4 have already confirmed that Jay will continue to film with the Woerdenweber family for the rest of the current series as well as the following run which is set to air later in the year.

Gogglebox airs Fridays at 9pm on Channel 4.
