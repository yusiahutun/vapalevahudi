Taylor Swift will be 'spoiled' by Travis Kelce with gifts this Christmas

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are planning their holidays, according to sources, which includes spending Christmas together.

Both Taylor Swift and Travis had packed schedules so far this the year, but sources close to the pair reveal they are eager to start new holiday traditions together. After Taylor’s Eras Tour concert in Brazil, the singer will be taking a short break for a few months before being back on stage in February. Meanwhile, Travis Kelce must still appear for a handful of Kansas City Chiefs matches. Fans speculate that Tay Tay will be spotted at the VIP box cheering for her beau during the holiday season, though.

Taylor Swift is going to be spoiled this Christmas, a source claims. It’s been said Travis Kelce has already started shopping for the music star.

The insider teased: “Travis has already started Christmas shopping and has some really special surprises [for Taylor] in store. Their connection is off the charts, and they can’t wait to celebrate together.”

Another source talked about Taylor and Travis’ serious approach to the relationship offering: “Taylor and Travis have the next few months completely laid out. They don’t want to start their relationship off with big gaps [in time spent apart]. They’re trying to be as much like a regular couple as possible.”

While Trav and Tay want to keep their relationship low-key like ‘regular people,’ the celebrity couple is not shying away from doing their fair bit when it comes to public declarations of love. Travis traveled to Argentina to see the Wildest Dream crooner perform live.

Tay then paid a tribute to the NFL star in the same concert by changing the lyrics of her popular song Karma, before running up to kiss him after the show.

“A romantic getaway”

Taylor and Travis’ plans for the New Year may include a romantic getaway says a close source. The insider adds the duo love spending time with their friends, but they are also looking forward to some alone time.

On the other hand, Travis has shared how Taylor’s best characteristic of being family-orientated is what he loves most about her. As per reports, Travis may visit Nashville to spend time at Taylor’s place for the holidays.

“Travis hasn’t been to Taylor’s Nashville place yet” and “he’s really looking forward to it,” the source added.
