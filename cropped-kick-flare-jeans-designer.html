The best designer kick flare jeans to invest in now

Is there any style of denim more flattering than cropped kick flare jeans?

    Looking for a new flattering denim cut? Since 2015 crop kick flare jeans have established themselves as a must-have denim style. 

    The crop kick flare has a high-waisted fit and slightly cropped hem, fitting close to the body through the knee into a subtle flared leg. This style is perfect for showing off your footwear or summer sandals, and are a fantastic alternative to skinny jeans (which according to Gen Z we can’t be caught dead in right now.) What’s more is they offer an indubitably more flattering fit than a baggy trouser, while still maintaining that relaxed feel.

    We list the 5 best pairs of cropped kick flare denim jeans to add to you cart this summer: 

    Anine Bing

    The Lara Jean from anine Bing is constructed from a cotton denim blend with subtle stretch. Its deep indigo wash features vintage-inspired highs and lows throughout accented with grinding at the front and back pockets. Finished with a raw-edge hem.

    Lara Jean, £199, FarFetch

    Citizens of Humanity 

    Citizens of Humanity presents the Isola pair this season, adding to its famed roster of Cali-cool jeans. In keeping with the brand's vintage-inspired aesthetic, this style is imbued with a faded wash and whiskering and shaped to finish at the ankle with a slight flare – a prime piece for a 70s-esque outfit. Better yet, the denim has a slight stretch for added comfort, perfect for days spent WFH or walking around your local park.

    Isola flared high-rise jeans, £300, Selfridges

    Khaite

    Khaite's jeans are modeled after founder Catherine Holstein's favorite vintage styles and meticulously tested for the perfect fit. Made from 'Vintage Blue' denim, this 'Vivian' pair has a flattering high-rise waist and cropped, bootcut legs. Wear yours with slim bodysuits and chunky sweaters alike.

    Vivian cropped high-rise bootcut jeans, £290, Net-A-Porter

    Veronica Beard

    Veronica Beard's 'Carly' jeans fit slim through the legs before kicking out to cropped, frayed hems. Cut to sit high on the waist, they're whiskered and faded for a broken-in look. Wear them with everything from crisp blouses to casual tees. 

    Carly frayed high-rise flared jeans, £120, Net-A-Porter

    Totême 

    The beauty of jeans is that you can style them for all sorts of events, and Totême's pair is a great example. Made from organic cotton in a soft blue hue, they're cut straight through the legs before kicking out to a flared ankle length. Tuck your top in to highlight the high-rise waist.
