Chris Evans-approved London Sock Company has a wedding edit for grooms

Attention all grooms (and wedding planning brides): London Sock Company, whose stylish socks have been worn by stars including Chris Evans, Tom Hiddleston and Benedict Cumberbatch, has a very chic wedding edit that you'll love.

The British brand's socks are designed in London, England and crafted at a family-run factory in Portugal. And there's design to fit every wedding theme, whether spring pastels, evening black tie weddings, or outdoor summer ceremonies.

The Simply Sartorial range ($22 / £14 a pair) alone comes in 33 shades to match pretty much any wedding color scheme.

From grooms to groomsmen, wedding socks he'll love

There's a cool selection of sock sets that are just perfect for grooms, best men and groomsmen (who could resist the set that comes with bottles of gin?).

Shop individual pairs or the set to gift, share or keep for yourself!

Wedding socks for kids

And if you're looking for kids' socks for the little ones in your wedding party, Little LSC's "snappy wee pairs" are mini replicas of the grown up luxury socks. They're fancy enough for a wedding and practical enough to wear later, whether to school or for weekend play dates.

So every guy in the wedding party will look perfectly put together... from head to toe!
