Kim Kardashian West Posts Revealing Snakeskin Print Bikini Photo on Instagram

  • Kim Kardashian took to Instagram to show off her newest bikini, which features a snakeskin print and is seriously revealing.
  • The KKW Beauty founder followed up the photo with a much more casual look in which she was wearing a sweatshirt.
  • Kim is no stranger to a naked look, and regularly surprises fans with her unexpected outfits.

If you're still shopping for bikinis this summer, then Kim Kardashian's latest Instagram photo might inspire you.

The makeup mogul took to Instagram to share a photo of herself chilling in a swimming pool, wearing an incredibly tiny, super chic, snakeskin-print bikini.

Kim followed up her risqué bikini look with a much more low-key photo. The reality TV icon showed off her favorite KKW Beauty look while wearing a baggy sweatshirt.

Earlier this week, Kim had a total Carrie Bradshaw moment when she channeled the iconic newspaper-print dress that the Sarah Jessica Parker's character wore in Season 3 of Sex and the City. The vintage Dior look, taken from John Galliano's era, featured the unforgettable print, this time as a wrap skirt. While Carrie opted for a figure-hugging dress, Kim wore a simple white tank top with the eye-catching skirt.
