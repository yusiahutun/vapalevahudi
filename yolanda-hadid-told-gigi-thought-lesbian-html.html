Yolanda Hadid once told Gigi she 'thought she was a lesbian'

Yolanda Hadid once admitted that she believed her supermodel daughter Gigi was 'a lesbian' because she loved to wear baggy basketball jerseys to school. 

The 55-year-old made the confession in a newly-unearthed clip from The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, which was released by Bravo as part of a mashup of scenes starring Gigi and her sister Bella, and sees Yolanda telling her daughter that she believed her tomboy-esque fashion choices in the third grade meant she was gay.

In the scene, which takes place in the kitchen of the family home in California, Gigi and her mother are discussing the budding model's desire to return to her club volleyball team - with Yolanda admitting that she fears the sport is going to make her daughter too 'bulky' to succeed as a catwalk star.     

Really? Yolanda Hadid once told daughter Gigi, pictured at a Tommy Hilfiger show in 2016, that she thought she was 'a lesbian' because she wore basketball jerseys in the third grade

Sports star: In an unearthed clip from The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, Yolanda tells a then-17-year-old Gigi (pictured in high school) that she couldn't play volleyball and model 

Controversy: The mashup clip, which was shared by Bravo on YouTube, has prompted some outcry from fans who have branded Yolanda 'scary' for how she talks to her daugthers 

'Volleyball is a very masculine sport. Modeling a very feminine thing,' Yolanda tells the then-17-year-old while preparing dinner for her family in the season three episode that aired in 2012.   

'She's gotta start building her career and that means sometimes we have to give up the things that we love to do,' Yolanda then explains in her testimonial, while noting that Gigi wants to be a model, but she is suddenly missing the time she spent playing volleyball.

Stage mom? In many of the scenes, Yolanda, now 55, is reminding Gigi to stick to her diet

'These girls they train four hours a day after school so their bodies are big and bulky. I mean, they eat like men. I wanted her to develop as a woman.'

The athletic-themed conversation then prompts Gigi, who is now 23, to recall her love of one particular sport: basketball. 

Gigi reminds her mother of a time in the third grade when she would wear nothing but baggy basketball clothes to school, saying: 'In third grade remember I used to wear basketball clothes to school every day? 

'Yes, a Laker jersey, right?' Yolanda remarks, before admitting: 'I thought my daughter was... a lesbian.' 

Since the episode was filmed seven years ago, Gigi has spoken again about her love of the sport back in the third grade, before she turned her talents towards volleyball, remarking in a previous interview: 'In third grade, I played basketball with the boys every day at lunch. 

'I had braces that were yellow and purple, and I wore full Laker uniforms to school.'

And while she has certainly grown beyond the days of club volleyball, she has found other ways to celebrate her love of sport that also involve her modeling career. 

In February 2014, just a few years after the RHOBH scenes were shot, Gigi took part in the Sports Illustrated Beach Volleyball Tournament in Miami, and came out on top as the winner of the entire competition. 

Then, in September 2016, she debuted an incredibly sporty collection of designs as part of her collaboration with Tommy Hilfiger - including a basketball jersey dress that was perhaps a tribute to her third grade days. 

Gigi, who has been linked to a number of A-list men since her mother made the comment about her sexuality, has also been seen sitting courtside at a number of basketball games in LA, as well as in New York City.

In 2015, she attended a game between the Brooklyn Nets and the New York Knicks alongside her then-boyfriend Cody Simpson, who she dated for two years. 

Following her relationship with Cody, Gigi had a brief romance with Joe Jonas, as well as a rumored fling with her close friend Lewis Hamilton. 

She then dated One Direction's Zayn Malik on-and-off until earlier this year. 

The comment is not the only controversial thing to come to light in the mashup, which has come under fire from several fans, who have slammed Yolanda as 'scary' and 'horrible' for the way she treats her daughters, particularly when it comes to Gigi's diet and weight. 

Throwback: Gigi, 23, and Bella, 22, were teenagers when they appeared alongside their mom, Yolanda Hadid (formerly Foster), on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills (pictured) 

Superstars: The sisters have become two of the most famous models in the world since their time on the show 

Then and now: Gigi was 17 years old and just starting out as a model when she first appeared in the show. She still has the same long blonde hair she had before she was famous 

Transformation: Bella looked completely different when she was 16 years old. Rumors have swirled for years that she's had plastic surgery, but she has vehemently denied the claims

In one scene, for example, Yolanda and Gigi are on set at one of her photo shoots. The teen is getting ready to celebrate her birthday, and all she is looking forward to is breaking her diet. 

'I am so excited for the food,' she tells her mom. 'Pretty much all of the food that we are eating is a like heart attack in a meal, but it's going to be so good.' 

Yolanda doesn't say anything about her daughter letting loose on her birthday, but she does warn her that she needs to stay thin in order to book work as a model. 

'You can have one night of being bad, right,' she tells Gigi. 'Then you gotta get back on your diet because, you know, in Paris and Milan they like the girls just a tad on the skinny side.'

Yolanda is still talking about Gigi's diet in the next clip from season four, but this time she is praising her for her hard work. '

'You are doing such a good job, Gigi,' she tells her while on a shoot. 'Aren't you proud? It's hard to exercise six days a week. It's hard to not eat any sugar, and it's hard to have to eat a salad every day, but now you see how it pays off.' 

Hard to watch: Before making her 'lesbian' comment, Yolanda discourages Gigi from playing volleyball because women who play the sport have bodies that are 'big and bulky'

Keeping it real? Yolonda, who was a famous model before she became a mom, is brutally honest with her daughters about what it takes to succeed in the cutthroat industry

Background: Bella and her younger brother, Anwar, are also in the scene, but the focus is Gigi wanting to play volleyball again 

Although Gigi admits that she sometimes hates working out, she agrees that it is all worth it because she is so happy to be a rising model. 

The majority of the footage focuses on Gigi and her budding modeling career, though a teenage Bella is present in some of the clips.

In one scene, Gigi and Bella are raiding their mother's closet before the eldest Hadid's trip to London for a shoot with Grazia magazine. 

'It's cute when you have girls the same size as you are,' Yolanda says in her testimonial. 'So now they can borrow things from me, but sometimes they don't bring it back. So that's not that cute.'

While Gigi proudly relays a conversation she had with a banker after depositing some work checks, Bella admits that she probably only has about $8 to her name. 

The next clip shows Gigi making a tearful speech at her graduation party, but the conversation once again returns to her diet after her mom surprises her with a cake featuring one of her modeling photos. 

Yikes: Before Gigi's birthday party, her mom warns her she has to get right back onto her diet because they like girls 'just a tad on the skinny side' in Milan and Paris

Constant reminder: Gigi's diet becomes a topic of discussion at her graduation party after Yolanda surprises her with a cake with one of her modeling pictures on it 

Proud: 'It's hard to exercise six days a week. It's hard to not eat any sugar, and it's hard to have to eat a salad every day, but now you see how it pays off,' Yolanda tells Gigi at one of her shoots

Evolution: Gigi walked the runway at Desigual during New York Fashion Week in 2012, just a few years into her career. She's pictured on the runway at the Isabel Marant show in February

Although Yolanda got her a cake in honor of her big move, it doesn't look like she wants her daughter to eat it. 

'This is the hardest for being on our diet,' she tells her daughter as she slices into her graduation cake. 

'I have to have a bite for good luck though,' Gigi says, but Yolanda doesn't seem convinced by her logic.

'Um,' she says, and things only get more awkward when Gigi forces her mom to have a slice of cake as well. 

Yolanda takes a minuscule bite before deciding she is done with it. 

'Gigi's in charge of her own diet, but to be on your best weight you gotta make the right choices,' the momager explains in her confessional. 

The following scene shows Yolanda moving Gigi into her first apartment in New York City. While Gigi was set on studying criminal psychology at The New School in the fall of 2013, her mom wasn't exactly on board.   

'I always felt she should take a year off and just work and make as much money as she can, and she didn't want to,' Yolanda tells the camera.

The mom also explains that Gigi was supposed to have a roommate, but that fell through because the other girl's parents wanted her to live in a dorm with other freshmen. 

Borrowing from mom: In one scene, Bella and Gigi are raiding their mother's closet. 'It's cute when you have girls the same size as you are,' Yolanda says in her testimonial

Choices : When Yolanda moves Gigi into her first apartment in New York, she admits she didn't want her to live in a college dorm because she wants her focused on modeling 

'I didn't want Gigi to live in a dorm because I wanted her to be in more of a work environment,' Yolanda says. 'Kids party and go out and get drunk and I wanted her to really focus on her career where she has to get up at five in the morning and go to work and shoot all day. You know, that was important to me.' 

Yolanda ended up getting her wish when Gigi suspended her studies to focus on her modeling career.  

Bella, on the other hand, wanted to focus on modeling from the get-go, but their mom was only willing to let her quit school if she was pulling in six figures. 

In the final scene in the clip, Yolanda and her daughters are hanging out in Gigi's apartment and helping Bella pick out photos for her modeling portfolio.  

'They are the black swan and the white swan, and obviously, as sisters that close in age there's always going to be competition, but Bella's going to do it her way,' Yolanda says of her daughters. 

'When somebody's looking for a brunette with blue eyes, Gigi's not gonna get the job. If they are looking for an all American girl, they're not going to hire Bella,' she adds. 

'I mean, it's just a big competitive business. If it's meant for them they are going to survive in it and be successful.'

The video has been viewed more than 3.5 million times, and some people took to the comments section to point out how different Bella looks at age 22 compared to when she was 16 years old. 

'I still don't get how Bella is influencing an entire generation as a model with a complete face of surgery and no one talks about it,' one person wrote, while someone else commented: 'Bella’s face is totally different.' 

Another added: 'And they try to say Bella had no plastic surgery to look how she does now.'  

Since Bella started her modeling career in 2013, rumors have swirled that she has had a nose job and other physical enhancements, but she has vehemently denied the claims. 

The clips also had some people feeling bad for Gigi and Bella because of the way Yolanda monitored their diets and 'pushed' them into modeling at the start of their careers. 

'Oh my god her mom is scary... I would run away if I was Gigi' one person commented, while someone else admitted: 'I never really cared for Gigi or Bella but after seeing this... I feel so bad for them.' 

'Wait this makes me really sad... like I'd have such horrible self-esteem if my mom treated me like that,' someone else wrote.

Gigi and Bella are now two of the most famous models in the world, and their younger brother Anwar, 19, is also a catwalk star. 

Fans of their mother defended her behavior, insisting that she was just being honest with them about how the modeling industry works. 

'If she didn't push, nothing would have come out the way that it did!' someone noted, while another person praised her parenting style. 

'Good for pushing her child. Now they are all successful,' the fan wrote.
