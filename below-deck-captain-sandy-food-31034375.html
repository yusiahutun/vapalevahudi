Below Deck captain Sandy had 'food flown to boat by plane' after guest demand

Below Deck star Sandra Yawn has revealed her wildest request to date after serving as Captain on the show.

In an exclusive interview with Daily Star, Sandy spoke about her experience on the boat and the time she had to get an aeroplane for a guest.

Sandy opened up on one of her most memorable requests and said: “We have had a request to charter aeroplanes specifically to fly certain food in because the charterers want it from the store, they want it fresh. If you have the money, we will make it happen. It’s all about the cash.”

“[Leah] was shocked, you could see pure joy in the photographs.”

Yawn gave some exciting information about their future plans, including when and where the wedding will take place: “It’s definitely going to be in February in Florida, the weather is incredible and it’ll be somewhere on a beach - no shoes!”

“If any co-stars are going to be invited…let’s see.”

Captain Sandy was speaking as part of the Women in Marine Day at the Southampton International Boat Show at Mayflower Park.
