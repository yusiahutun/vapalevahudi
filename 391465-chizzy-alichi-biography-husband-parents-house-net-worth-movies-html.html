Acting career

Chigozie Stephanie Alichi's love for acting was evident as a child. However, she began her career by registering with Actors Guild in Nigeria in 2010. After attending auditions, she started featuring in minor roles before gaining recognition in Nollywood.

As a result of her exemplary performance in the movie, Chigozie won the best new actress of the year award. So far, the beautiful, talented, and creative actress has acted in over seventy best-selling films.
