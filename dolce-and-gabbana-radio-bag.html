Dolce &amp; Gabbana's Radio Purse

My favorite thing to watch in New York City is people who walk around with a boom box blasting. It feels so retro and at the same time, shows such an unabashed joy for music—they love what they're rocking out to so much, they can't imagine other people won't revel in it as well. Dolce & Gabbana brought that tune love into a boom box handbag—speakers and all—for Resort 2017. But you won't have to wait for summer to end to get your hands on it. The DG Dolce & Gabbana Radio Bag, inspired by classic '80s boom boxes, is available at www.dolcegabbana.it now. The bag is also fully functional: compatible with smartphone devices, it features working speakers. I'm thinking it will pair perfectly with a little old school Prince.
