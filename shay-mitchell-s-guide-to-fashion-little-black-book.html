Watch Shay Mitchell Talk Paris Fashion Week, Stylist Monica Rose and Favorite Outfits

Watch as actress Shay Mitchell dives into what fashion means to her as she prepares for Paris Fashion Week for her exclusive series with BAZAAR.com, Little Black Book. In this episode, Shay takes us through her fashion journey—from her best looks to her most regrettable— and to a fitting with her "fairy god sister" stylist, Monica Rose.

Shop Shay's Closet

"I've always been obsessed with fashion, " Mitchell tells BAZAAR.com. Now that Shay gets to attend events and red carpets, she seizes the opportunity to have fun with her looks. "I really don't take it too seriously because I think that it's meant to be fun."

Mitchell relies on Hollywood stylist Monica Rose to help pull together her chic looks. The duo first met on a photoshoot this year and have been making magic ever since. "Monica is so talented in the things she pulls and how she puts them together, and she is truly a magician when it comes to clothing, and fashion, and styling," Mitchell gushes.

Shay has lots of fun trying on the looks in preparation for fashion shows and street style moments. Rose says the clothes have a lot of impact on her client. "I think what I love the most about styling you is that you become the character," she says. "I think that's what so great about fashion is you play with it and have fun with it."

Check back next week for the second episode, where Shay will reveal even more of her secrets.
