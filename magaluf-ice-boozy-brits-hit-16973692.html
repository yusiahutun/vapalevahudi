Magaluf on ICE: Boozy Brits hit ski town for 2 pints and strip clubs

Groups of rowdy lads are flocking to Bulgaria to go “on the piste” in the small town of Borovets.

But instead of hitting the slopes, they hit the booze as they stagger in and out of the seven strip clubs.

Drinks cost just £1 for shots and £2 a pint during Happy Hour – and there are more escort girls “than in Soho”.

One small website advert had dozens of listings for hookers of all ages and shapes and sizes.

Now fed-up skiers have complained on TripAdvisor about the stag parties “ruining” Borovets.

One moaned: “It is a 95% man resort with big groups of men getting drunk from 3pm. “I was shocked to see more sex and adult show/shops than in Soho.”

Another added: “If you’re a young lad who likes to get very drunk on questionable shots and eat big portions of pub grub, this might be the place for you.
