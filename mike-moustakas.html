Biography

Mike Moustakas was born in Los Angeles, CA on September 11, 1988. Third baseman who made his MLB debut for the Kansas City Royals in 2011 and hit 20 home runs for the team in 2012. He helped the Royals win the 2014 AL Pennant before falling to the San Francisco Giants in a thrilling 7-game World Series. He then won a World Series Championship with the Royals the following year. Outfielder Jeff Francoeur was his teammate on the Royals. He played baseball at Chatsworth High School in California.
