James Van Der Beek and family show off their matching red string Kabbalah bracelets

Starting them young! James Van Der Beek and wife Kimberly show off their babies' string Kabbalah bracelets

The Dawson's Creek alum has been a devotee of Kabbalah - a mystical celebrity adored offshoot of Judaism -  for many years.

In fact, the 35-year-old married his wife Kimberly in Israel in 2010 in a traditional Jewish ceremony, then they spent their honeymoon in the Holy Land.

And now the pair, who are thought to have met at prayer meetings, are extending their beliefs to their young family by having them wear the red string Kabbalah bracelet.

The family that prays together: James Van Der Veek and his wife Kimberly showed off red Kabbalah bracelets tied around the wrists of their whole family at the 16th Biggest Baby Shower Ever in New York on Friday

The whole family sported the recognisable string at the 16th Biggest Baby Shower Ever in New York on Friday.

Their daughter Oliva, two, who was wearing a pretty purple dress, displayed the bracelet on her left wrist and walked barefoot on the red carpet.

But one-year-old Joshua was not to be left out and could be seen unwittingly showing off his religious paraphernalia as he pointed his daddy's attention to the ceiling.

Dawson's mini me: Joshua showed off his bracelet as he larked around with daddy

We get it Kimberly: Just in case it hadn't been noted, Mrs Van Der Beek profferred her wrist to the camera

Olivia's dress tied into the vivid pattern of her businesswoman mother's paisley dress.

Kimberly, who wore her red hair down, wore her own bracelet, of course.

Other famous celebrity devotees of Kabbalah include Demi Moore, her ex Ashton Kutcher, Madonna and her brood, and even Lindsay Lohan flirted with the faith.

Gorgeous family: The Van Der Beeks are a good looking bunch, and Joshua is the image of his father, James

Looking to a higher power? Little Joshua seemed fascinated by something going on above

Show of faith: Van Der Beek married in Kabbalah ceremony and is a strong believer in this Jewish mystical offshoot

Roseanne Barr, Sandra Bernhard, Anthony Kiedis, Mick Jagger, Jerry Hall, Lucy Liu, Alex Rodriguez, Rosie O'Donnell, Naomi Campbell, Donna Karan, Elizabeth Taylor, Mischa Barton, Britney Spears, Paris Hilton and Nicole Richie have also been linked.

James and Kimberly's small wedding was held at a Kabbalah Center in Tel Aviv and performed by Rabbi Yehuda Berg, the son of the founder of the Kabbalah Center in Jerusalem.

'Best day of my life so far,' he Tweeted after the August wedding, before adding, 'The woman I love did me the honor of [becoming] my wife today … I look forward to earning her for the rest of my life.'
