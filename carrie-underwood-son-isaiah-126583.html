Aww!Carrie Underwood Wishes Son Isaiah a Happy Birthday With Sweet Instagram Photo

Let’s just call Carrie Underwood supermom!

The country singer wished her adorable son, Isaiah, a happy second birthday on Instagram on Monday, Feb. 27 — and her words about the little boy will literally melt your heart!

"Where did two years go? My angel is getting so big! On one hand, it's so fun to see him learn and grow but on the other, it's all happening so fast! Happy birthday, little monkey! You are my world…my heart…and I thank God for you each and every day! The two years you have been on this earth have been the best of our lives! Mommy and Daddy love you so much!" she captioned a cuter than cute photo of Isaiah playing with a birthday balloon.

Carrie married her pro hockey player hubby, Mike Fisher, in 2010, and the two welcomed their little boy in February 2015.

In a recent interview, the 33 year-old revealed she’s taking a break from music to be a mom to Isaiah.

"We just wrapped up the Storyteller Tour and I feel like my life right now, creatively, is kind of a blank space," she told the People/Entertainment Weekly Network at the Grammys on Sunday, Feb. 12.

And she isn't mad about it! "I’m really excited about that because you don’t know where you’re going to go,” the former American Idol winner continued. “I’m nervous because I want whatever I do next to be amazing, so I’m kind of taking a little break right now and being a mom and being a wife and going to see some hockey games to see my husband play. I’m kind of waiting for lightning to strike and go: ‘Okay, it’s time to get creative and work on new music!’"

He's such a great dad and he's so supportive of what I do and I'm supportive of him and we just love our little family."
