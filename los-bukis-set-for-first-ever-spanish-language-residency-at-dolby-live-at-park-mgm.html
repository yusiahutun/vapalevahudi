Los Bukis Set For First-Ever Spanish Language Residency At Dolby Live At Park MGM

LAS VEGAS (CelebrityAccess) – Los Bukis, the legendary Mexican band that enjoyed a successful reunion tour in 2021, has made a historic announcement by launching the first Spanish-language residency on the Las Vegas Strip. This marks a significant milestone in their illustrious career, as the iconic group is set to grace the stage at Dolby Live at Park MGM starting on May 3.

In collaboration with Live Nation and MGM Resorts International, the residency, titled “Los Bukis: The Las Vegas Residency,” is specially tailored for the intimate setting of Dolby Live, with a capacity of 5,200 seats. Overseeing the residency from Live Nation is SVP Concerts Las Vegas Sid Greenfeig, along with support from Latin music executives SVP, Global Touring Hans Schafer, and Global Touring Promoter Jorge Garcia.

The ticket presale is scheduled to commence on Wednesday (January 31) at 10 am local time, followed by the general on-sale on Friday (February 2) at 10 am local time. The announced residency dates cover performances in May, July, and September:

May: 3-4, 8, 10-11
July: 12-13, 17, 19-20
September: 13-14, 18, 20-21

This historic Las Vegas residency announcement further solidifies Los Bukis’ enduring influence and cultural significance in the music industry. Fans and music enthusiasts alike can anticipate a series of unforgettable performances in the heart of the entertainment capital of the world.
