HFM's Tuesday Shoesday! Dune London's dreamy bridal shoes

The stunning range starts from £20

    Calling all brides-to-be! If you are getting married this year, or even if you just happen to have a penchant for fairy-tale shoes, you need to check out The Wedding Collection by Dune London – the perfect candidate for our Tuesday Shoesday! Every week here at HFM we present a new offering that focuses on the wonderful world of shoes. This week - Dune London is in the spotlight as the brand presents a beautiful 15-piece ivory satin footwear collection alongside an extensive range of matching clutch bags and accessories which will be available online from 24 January.

    The Wedding Collection by Dune London is all kinds of dreamy 

    The specialised footwear company has amped up their signature shoe designs that we all know and love and added that extra bit of sparkle – the designers have reworked in luxury ivory satin to include a full range of statement stamps to suit a variety of bridal looks. If you are a modern bride or envisage yourself as Cinderella type of gal, there is something for everyone.

    There's an abundance of styles to cater for every bride 

    The Wedding Collection features stunning bespoke embellishments, including pearl brooch details and floral applique trims that would make the dreamiest 'shoefies' on our Instagram feeds. Not only do they look the business, but they have been cleverly designed with latex and memory foam sock linings which will give you the most comfortable fit ever – and means every bride can get down on the dancefloor without wincing.

    We love the pearl brooch detailing 

    It's hard to pick a favourite but we are particularly loving the extra-fabulous, limited edition satin court shoes that have snazziest heel we have ever seen – winding blooms with delicate rhinestones and pearls. Bridesmaid dressing needn't be stressful either– the collection also offers a wider selection of footwear and accessories for the whole bridal party – including kitten and high heels in 8 luxury satin shades for just £80.
